# SAR Intelligence — Regulatory-Change Slice (2025–2026)

The SAR / reporting / advisory-typology slice of the FinCEN Updates corpus. Full ledger:
**`../fincen/references/regulatory-updates-2025-2026.md`**; SAR/CTR detail:
`../fincen/references/sar-ctr-reporting.md`. Verify all status on FinCEN.gov.

## The headline SAR change
- **2025-10-09 — SAR FAQs (four), joint with FRB/FDIC/NCUA/OCC** `FAQ`. Clarify SAR
  requirements to **stop needless resource expenditure** on defensive/duplicative filings and
  over-frequent continuing-activity reviews. Interpretive of 31 CFR 1020.320 et al.;
  31 U.S.C. 5318(g).
  - *Impact:* institutions may **right-size** SAR programs (e.g., continuing-activity cadence) —
    but **document the FAQ basis**; an examiner still tests reasonableness.

## Reporting reminders
- **CTR threshold $10,000** (31 CFR 1010.311); **structuring** to evade it (31 U.S.C. 5324) is a
  core SAR red flag.
- **GTO overlays** drop the CTR threshold far below $10k for covered MSBs/banks (Southwest
  Border, Minnesota) — structuring **around the lowered threshold** is the typology to watch.
  See `../fincen/references/gto-tracker.md`.

## Advisories that add SAR red-flag typologies
- **2026-06-05 — Non-work-authorized populations & employers** `Advisory` — payroll/off-book
  employment red flags.
- **2026-05-11 / 2026-06-11 — 2026 FIFA World Cup human-trafficking notice** (+ Spanish)
  `Notice` — HT financial indicators (funnel accounts, third-party travel/lodging) for the
  event window.
- **2026-05-11 — IRGC ML alert** `Alert` — Iran/IRGC laundering indicators; **also an OFAC**
  exposure (route to `/glaw-fincen-ofac`).
- **2025-09-08 — Financially motivated sextortion notice** `Notice` — extortion-payment
  indicators (often crypto).
- **2025-11-13 — Sinaloa Cartel: 10 MX gambling establishments = primary ML concern** `Notice`
  (31 U.S.C. 5318A).

## Information sharing (feeds SAR detection)
- **2026-06-12 — Eliminate fraud via information sharing** `Advisory` — lean on **314(b)**
  (31 CFR 1010.540) for fraud, not just ML/TF.
- **2025-09-05 — Cross-border information sharing** `Advisory`.

## Analyst takeaways
1. **Anomaly ≠ red flag ≠ finding.** Tie each flag to a typology + the record line.
2. The 2025 FAQs let you defend a **leaner, smarter** SAR program — cite them.
3. This seat produces a **suspicious-activity analysis, not a filed SAR** — the BSA officer
   owns the filing decision.
