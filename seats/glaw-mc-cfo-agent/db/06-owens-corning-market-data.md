## Roofing Market Intelligence

| Year | New Construction | Weather | Repair | Total |
|------|-----------------|---------|--------|-------|
| 2015-2020 avg | 21 | 40 | 75 | 136 |
| 2021 | 28 | 41 | 90 | 159 |
| 2022 | 26 | 41 | 81 | 148 |

**Key takeaway:** 80%+ of roofing demand is non-discretionary (weather + repair), making the market resilient to economic cycles.

### Shingle Product Mix Evolution
- **95%** of roofing demand is now laminate shingles (vs. strip shingles)
- Laminates command higher price point → reinforces higher-value roofing systems
- Components business growing with margins similar to shingles

### Contractor Engagement Model
- **>30,000 contractors** in Owens Corning Contractor Network (OCCN)
- Duration Mix (premium product) growing 2015-2025
- **17% CAGR** in contractor additions 2015-2025
- Pink Advantage Dealer Program creates pull-through demand

## Capital Allocation Strategy
- Maintain investment grade: 2-3x net debt / adjusted EBITDA
- Return free cash flow via dividends + repurchases
- **$4.6B returned to shareholders since 2019**
- Tripled quarterly dividend since 2019
- 31% of outstanding shares repurchased 2019-2025
- Target: 50% of FCF to shareholders over time

## Strategic Priorities for Roofing Companies to Note

1. **Contractor engagement models** — loyalty programs create stickiness and data
2. **Product mix upgrade** — strips → laminates → premium systems (higher margin)
3. **Components business** — accessories and roofing components growing as profit center
4. **Non-discretionary demand** — 80%+ of market is replacement/weather = defensive moat
5. **Technology investment** — VR training, digital customer portal, analytics/AI
6. **Sustainability** — growing requirement for ESG reporting even in construction

- $3.7B revenue, 24% average EBITDA margin

- Data centers driving commercial demand

## Doors Segment (Acquisition Case Study)
- $2.1B revenue, path to 20% EBITDA margin
- $200M in total cost synergies ($125M original + $75M additional)
- Network optimization, automation, operational efficiency
- Example of vertical integration across components + systems
