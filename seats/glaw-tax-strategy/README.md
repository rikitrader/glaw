# tax-strategy

Proactive, **legal** tax-minimization & wealth-structuring advisor — the "how Meta and Musk
actually do it" playbook, done with real economic substance. The planning counterpart to the
reactive `tax-compliance` skill.

## What it does

Profiles a client (W-2 earner / SMB owner / startup founder / real-estate investor /
multinational / UHNW), then maps the highest-impact legal levers and outputs a ranked plan with
implementation steps, documentation, and honest risk/benefit — leading with the mundane, bulletproof
moves and naming the folklore it won't recommend.

Covered: entity & holdco design (S-corp split, C-corp + **QSBS §1202**, management company), IP
licensing & transfer pricing (§482, FDDEI, the realistic limits post-GILTI/Pillar Two), personal
wealth tactics (buy-borrow-die + §1014 step-up, equity-comp timing, charitable, GRAT/IDGT/SLAT/
dynasty trusts), deferral/credits/depreciation (retirement incl. DB cash-balance, QBI §199A, cost
seg + bonus, R&D, Augusta, OZ, 1031), and state/PR/offshore residency.

## Scope & limits

Legal **planning** only — it refuses evasion and rejects substance-free structures. Informational,
not legal/tax advice for a specific situation; engage a licensed CPA/tax attorney. Current-law
anchors verified live (June 2026): QSBS §1202 OBBBA tiers ($15M/$75M, 3/4/5-yr), GILTI→NCTI &
FDII→FDDEI (~14%), economic-substance §7701(o) (20/40% penalty), OECD Pillar Two (15%, >€750M).
Rates and limits change — verify on IRS.gov before relying.
