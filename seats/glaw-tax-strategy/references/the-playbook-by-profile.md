# The Playbook, by Client Profile

Match the client to a row, then go deep in the named reference. Lead with the high-$/low-risk
levers; the exotic stuff is the last 10%. Verify all figures per SKILL.md Step 1a.

## W-2 high earner (no business)
Limited levers — but real ones:
- **Max tax-advantaged accounts**: 401(k) (+ catch-up), **mega-backdoor Roth** if the plan allows
  after-tax + in-plan conversion, **HSA** (triple tax-free), backdoor Roth IRA.
- **Equity comp timing** (if RSUs/options) — see personal-wealth-tactics.
- **Charitable bunching** into a **donor-advised fund** in high-income years; donate appreciated
  stock (avoid the gain + deduct FMV).
- **State residency** — relocate to a no-tax state before a bonus/IPO/vesting cliff.
- **Side business?** — if real, unlocks the SMB toolkit below.

## SMB owner / professional practice
The richest mundane savings:
- **Entity choice** — S-corp election to split reasonable salary vs distribution (saves SE tax),
  while preserving **QBI §199A** 20% deduction (watch the SSTB phase-out for professionals).
- **Retirement** — **solo 401(k)**, SEP, or a **defined-benefit / cash-balance plan** (can deduct
  six figures/yr for older high-earners).
- **Augusta rule §280A(g)** — rent your home to your business ≤14 days/yr, tax-free to you.
- **Accountable plan** — reimburse home-office/auto/cell tax-free.
- **Hire your kids** (real work, reasonable wages) — shifts income to low brackets.
- **Cost segregation / §179 / bonus depreciation** on real estate and equipment.
- **Management company / holdco** if multiple entities — see entity-and-holdco.

## High-growth startup founder
- **QSBS §1202** is the crown jewel — C-corp stock held 5 yrs → up to the greater of **$15M or
  10× basis** excluded (post-7/4/2025 stock; tiered 50/75/100% at 3/4/5 yrs). Plan the C-corp and
  issuance date around it.
- **83(b) election** within 30 days of restricted-stock grant.
- **QSBS stacking** — gift shares to non-grantor trusts / family so each gets its own §1202 cap.
- **C-corp vs passthrough** — re-model post-OBBBA; QSBS often tips founders to C-corp.

## Real-estate investor
- **Cost segregation + bonus depreciation** — front-load depreciation; **bonus is back to 100%**
  for property placed in service after Jan 19, 2025 (OBBBA — verify).
- **1031 like-kind exchange** — defer gain into replacement property.
- **Real Estate Professional Status (REPS)** or short-term-rental loophole — turn passive losses
  active to offset other income.
- **Refinance, don't sell** — pull out tax-free cash via debt (a "buy-borrow" cousin).
- **Opportunity Zones** — defer + potentially exclude gains.

## Multinational / IP-heavy business
- IP/licensing + **transfer pricing (§482)**, **FDDEI** (export incentive), and holding-company
  design — **only with real substance**; faces GILTI/NCTI + Pillar Two. See
  ip-licensing-and-transfer-pricing. Not a small-business move.

## UHNW / business exit / estate
- **Buy-borrow-die** + **§1014 step-up**, equity diversification via exchange funds.
- **Estate freeze trusts** — GRAT, IDGT sale, SLAT — move appreciation out of the taxable estate.
- **Charitable** — CRT (income + deduction + defer gain), CLAT, **private foundation** or DAF.
- **Use the lifetime gift/estate exemption** before it changes; **dynasty trusts** in friendly
  states (SD, NV) for multi-generational shelter.
- See personal-wealth-tactics.
