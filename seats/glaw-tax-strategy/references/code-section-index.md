# IRC Code-Section Index — the deductions & provisions the wealthy actually use

A working index of the Internal Revenue Code sections behind real tax planning. **This is a map,
not a guarantee** — every line still requires eligibility, substance (§7701(o)), and current-year
verification (Step 1a). Grouped by function.

## Rate & character of income (the foundation)
- **§1(h)** — preferential **long-term capital gains / qualified dividends** rates (0/15/20%) vs
  ordinary. The whole game is converting ordinary income into LTCG and deferring realization.
- **§1411** — 3.8% **net investment income tax** (plan around it).
- **§1202** — **QSBS** exclusion (see entity-and-holdco; OBBBA tiers, $15M/$75M).
- **§1045** — rollover of QSBS gain into new QSBS.
- **§1014** — **basis step-up at death** (the engine of "buy-borrow-die").
- **§1031** — like-kind exchange (real property) deferral.
- **§1044 / §1400Z-2** — Opportunity Zone deferral & exclusion.

## Business income & deductions
- **§162** — ordinary & necessary **business expenses** (the master deduction).
- **§199A** — **QBI** 20% passthrough deduction (SSTB limits).
- **§179** — immediate expensing of equipment.
- **§168(k)** — **bonus depreciation** (100% for property placed in service after 1/19/2025 per OBBBA — verify).
- **§168 / cost segregation** — accelerated depreciation by component life.
- **§41** — **R&D credit**; **§174** — R&D expensing/amortization.
- **§280A(g)** — **Augusta rule** (rent home to business ≤14 days, tax-free).
- **§119 / §132** — tax-free fringe benefits (lodging, de minimis, working condition).
- **§465 / §469** — at-risk & passive-activity-loss limits (REPS exception for real estate).
- **§163(j)** — business interest limitation.
- **§1361–1379** — S-corp rules (salary/distribution split, saves SE tax).
- **§701–777** — partnership taxation (special allocations, §704(b), §754 step-up).
- **§11 / §243** — C-corp 21% rate; **dividends-received deduction** (intercompany).
- **§482** — transfer pricing (arm's-length intercompany charges).

## International (large/operating businesses)
- **§951A / "NCTI" (ex-GILTI)** — current tax on foreign intangible income (~14% floor post-OBBBA).
- **§250 / "FDDEI" (ex-FDII)** — export incentive deduction (~14% effective).
- **§245A** — participation-exemption dividend.
- **§367 / §936/§933** — outbound transfers; Puerto Rico residents' income.
- **§877A** — expatriation/exit tax.

## Retirement & deferral (huge for earners — see retirement playbook)
- **§401(k)/§401(a)** — qualified plans; **§415(c)** total-additions limit ($72k 2026).
- **§408 / §408A** — traditional & **Roth IRA**; **§408(d)** backdoor mechanics.
- **§402(e)(4)** — **NUA** (net unrealized appreciation on employer stock).
- **§409A** — nonqualified deferred comp rules.
- **§412 / §404** — defined-benefit / cash-balance funding & deduction (six-figure deferrals).
- **§72(t)** — early-withdrawal exception (SEPP); **§223** — HSA (triple tax-free).

## Equity compensation
- **§422** — ISOs (no ordinary tax at exercise; AMT preference under **§56**).
- **§83** — property for services; **§83(b)** election (30-day); **§83(i)** deferral.
- **§1202/§1045** — QSBS interplay with founder stock.

## Charitable (deduction + gain avoidance)
- **§170** — charitable deduction (appreciated stock at FMV; AGI limits by asset/charity type).
- **§664** — **CRT**; **§170(f) / §2522** — **CLAT**; **§4940–4945** — private foundation excise/rules.
- **§4966–4967** — donor-advised funds.

## Estate, gift & trust (wealth transfer)
- **§2010** — unified gift/estate **exemption**; **§2505** — gift exemption; **§2631** — GST exemption.
- **§2503(b)** — annual gift exclusion; **§2702 / GRAT** — estate-freeze annuity trusts.
- **§671–679** — **grantor-trust** rules (IDGT; grantor pays tax = tax-free gift); **§677** SLAT.
- **§2032 / §2032A** — alternate valuation / special-use valuation.
- **§101** — life-insurance proceeds income-tax-free (ILIT).

## Anti-abuse / the rules that bound everything (see guardrails)
- **§7701(o)** — economic substance (strict-liability 20/40% penalty, §6662(b)(6)/(i)).
- **§482** — transfer pricing reallocation.
- **§531–537 / §541** — accumulated-earnings & personal-holding-company penalties (C-corp traps).
- **§6662(e)** — transfer-pricing penalty; **§6111/§6707A** — reportable-transaction disclosure.
- **§267** — related-party loss/expense disallowance.
