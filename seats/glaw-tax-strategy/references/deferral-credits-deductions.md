# Deferral, Credits & Depreciation (the mundane levers that actually move the needle)

The unglamorous core of real tax savings. Verify current limits/rates per SKILL.md Step 1a.

## Retirement & deferral (often the single biggest legal lever for earners)

| Vehicle | Who | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **401(k) + employer match** | Employees | Pre-tax deferral + match; add catch-up if 50+ |
| **Mega-backdoor Roth** | Employees whose plan allows after-tax + in-plan Roth conversion | Get tens of $k/yr into Roth beyond normal limits |
| **Backdoor Roth IRA** | High earners over the income limit | Non-deductible IRA → Roth conversion |
| **SEP-IRA / Solo 401(k)** | Self-employed / SMB | Large deductible contributions on business income |
| **Defined-benefit / cash-balance plan** | Older high-earning owners | Deduct **six figures/year**; the heavy-hitter for 45+ professionals |
| **HSA** | HDHP holders | **Triple** tax-free (deduct, grow, withdraw for medical); stealth retirement account |

## QBI — §199A (passthrough owners)

20% deduction on qualified business income from passthroughs. Watch the **SSTB** limitation
(service businesses — law, health, consulting, finance) phasing out above income thresholds;
entity structure and income smoothing can preserve it. OBBBA made §199A permanent — verify
thresholds.

## Depreciation (businesses & real estate)

- **Bonus depreciation** — **100%** first-year expensing for qualified property placed in service
  after **Jan 19, 2025** (OBBBA restored 100% — verify). Massive front-loaded deduction.
- **§179 expensing** — immediate expensing up to the annual cap (verify; OBBBA raised it).
- **Cost segregation** — engineering study that reclassifies building components into 5/7/15-yr
  lives so they qualify for bonus/accelerated depreciation — large first-year deductions on real
  estate. (Cross-ref `roofer-accounting` / `institutional-finance` for the study mechanics.)

## Credits (dollar-for-dollar — better than deductions)

- **R&D credit (§41)** — for businesses doing qualified development (software, product,
  process); can offset payroll tax for startups. High-value and under-claimed.
- **Work Opportunity, energy/§48, EV, and industry-specific credits** — screen for fit.

## Owner-specific deductions

- **Augusta rule §280A(g)** — rent your residence to your business ≤14 days/yr; **tax-free** to
  you, deductible to the business (document a real business purpose + market rate).
- **Accountable plan** — reimburse home office, mileage, cell, travel tax-free to the owner.
- **Hire your children** — real work at reasonable wages shifts income to their low bracket
  (and a sole-prop family business may avoid payroll tax on the kids' wages).
- **Health/fringe** — HSA, §125 cafeteria plans, QSEHRA/ICHRA for small employers.

## Investment-level deferral

- **1031 like-kind exchange** — defer real-estate gain into replacement property.
- **Opportunity Zones** — defer (and potentially partially exclude) capital gains reinvested in a
  Qualified Opportunity Fund; verify current deferral deadlines.
- **Installment sales (§453)** — spread gain over years (avoid abusive monetized variants).
- **Tax-loss harvesting** + long-term holding (>1 yr) for everyone.
