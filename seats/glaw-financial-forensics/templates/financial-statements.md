# CPA-Format Statement Templates

Use these layouts for the Phase-3 deliverables. Keep them professional and consistent. Model
real format on `knowledge/sample-contractor-financial-statement-stambaugh-ness.md` and
`knowledge/nrca-2021-22-audited-financial-statements.md`. Every statement carries a header:
**Entity · Statement name · Period · Basis (cash/accrual/hybrid) · "Reconstructed from source
documents — unaudited"**.

---

## 1. Income Statement (Profit & Loss)
```
[ENTITY] — Income Statement
For the period [start] to [end]  ·  Basis: [cash/accrual]  ·  Reconstructed, unaudited

REVENUE
  Sales Revenue ......................... $ ____
  Service Revenue ....................... $ ____
  Subscription Revenue .................. $ ____
  Merchant Deposits (net) ............... $ ____
  Other Income .......................... $ ____
    Total Revenue ....................... $ ____
COST OF GOODS SOLD
  Materials ............................. $ ____
  Subcontractors ........................ $ ____
  Direct Labor .......................... $ ____
    Total COGS .......................... $ ____
  GROSS PROFIT .......................... $ ____   (__%)
OPERATING EXPENSES
  [each category] ....................... $ ____
    Total OpEx .......................... $ ____
  OPERATING INCOME ...................... $ ____
OTHER INCOME / (EXPENSE)
  Interest Expense ...................... $ (___)
  NET INCOME ............................ $ ____   (__%)
```

## 2. Balance Sheet
```
[ENTITY] — Balance Sheet · As of [date] · Reconstructed, unaudited
ASSETS                              LIABILITIES & EQUITY
 Current:                            Current:
  Cash ................. $ ____       Accounts Payable .... $ ____
  Accounts Receivable .. $ ____       Credit Cards ........ $ ____
  Inventory ............ $ ____       Payroll/Sales Tax ... $ ____
  Underbillings ........ $ ____       Overbillings ........ $ ____
 Fixed:                              Long-term:
  Fixed Assets ......... $ ____       Loans / Notes ....... $ ____
  Less: Accum Depr ..... $ (___)     EQUITY
                                       Owner's Capital ..... $ ____
                                       Retained Earnings ... $ ____
 TOTAL ASSETS ......... $ ====       TOTAL LIAB + EQUITY .. $ ====
                            (ASSETS must equal LIAB + EQUITY)
```

## 3. Cash Flow Statement (indirect)
```
OPERATING:  Net Income ± non-cash ± Δworking capital ............ $ ____
INVESTING:  asset purchases/sales .............................. $ ____
FINANCING:  loans drawn/repaid, owner contrib/draws ............ $ ____
NET CHANGE IN CASH ............................................. $ ____
Beginning Cash + Net Change = Ending Cash  (must = bank ending = BS cash)
```

## 4. Trial Balance
`Account | Debit | Credit` — every account; **Σ Debit = Σ Credit** (show totals).

## 5. General Ledger Summary
Per account: opening, each posting (date · ref · debit/credit · source-line), closing.

## 6-9. Analyses
- **Revenue Analysis:** by stream, by month (table + trend).
- **Expense Analysis:** by category & vendor, $ and % of revenue, trend.
- **Monthly Profitability:** month x (Revenue, COGS, GP, OpEx, Net, margin%).
- **YTD Summary:** cumulative through period end vs prior if available.

## 10. Executive Financial Dashboard
```
KPIs:  Revenue | Gross Margin% | Net Margin% | EBITDA | Cash | Runway(mo)
Ratios: Current | Quick | Debt | D/E
SCORES: Financial Health __/100 · IRS Readiness __/100 · Bookkeeping __/100 ·
        Fraud Risk __/100 · Cash Flow Stability __/100
Top Risks (ranked) + one-line CFO call-to-action.
```

## Construction add-on — WIP / Over-Under Billing Schedule
Per job: `Contract $ | Est. Cost | Cost-to-date | % Complete (cost/cost) | Earned Rev |
Billed | Over(+)/Under(-) billing`. Totals reconcile to BS accounts 1400/2400. (KB: CICPAC,
Peterson.)
