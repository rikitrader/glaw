# Equity Issuance & Securities Compliance

**Every issuance of stock, units, options, SAFEs, or convertible notes is a securities offering**
that must (a) be **registered** or (b) fit a **registration exemption**, AND comply with **state
Blue Sky** law. Structure to *qualify* — never to evade. Verify current rules per Step 1a; hand
real raises to `pe-vc-counsel`.

## Federal exemptions (the common ones)

| Exemption | Use | Key conditions |
|---|---|---|
| **Reg D Rule 506(b)** | Most private raises | Unlimited accredited + ≤35 non-accredited (sophisticated); **no general solicitation**; file **Form D** |
| **Reg D Rule 506(c)** | Private raise with marketing | Accredited **only**, with **verification**; general solicitation allowed; Form D |
| **§4(a)(2)** | Private placement (statutory) | No public offering; the basis Reg D safe-harbors |
| **Rule 701** | **Employee/founder equity** (private co.) | Compensatory awards under a written plan; annual caps; disclosure over threshold |
| **Reg CF** | Crowdfunding | Via a funding portal; annual cap; Form C |
| **Reg A / A+** | "Mini-IPO" | Tiers I/II; qualified offering circular |

- **Form D** filed with the SEC within **15 days** of first sale; **Blue Sky** notice filings +
  fees in each state where investors reside.
- **Accredited investor** — income/net-worth or qualifying-credential tests; 506(c) requires
  *reasonable steps to verify* (not just a checkbox).

## Founder / employee equity mechanics
- **§83(b) election — 30-day hard deadline** on restricted stock: elect to be taxed at grant
  (low) so all appreciation is capital gain. Miss it = ordinary income at each vesting.
- **409A valuation** — set option strike at FMV via an independent 409A appraisal (refresh
  annually / on material events) to avoid punitive §409A tax.
- **ISO vs NSO** — ISOs (employees, AMT preference, holding rules) vs NSOs (anyone, ordinary
  income at exercise). **Rule 701** covers the offering exemption for the plan.
- **Cap table hygiene** — stock ledger, board approval for every issuance, option-pool sizing,
  vesting schedules; coordinate **QSBS** qualification with `tax-strategy`.

## Issuance checklist (do every time)
1. Board (and any required class) **approval** + minutes/consent.
2. Confirm the **exemption** (506(b)/(c), 701, 4(a)(2)) and that facts fit (solicitation,
   accreditation, caps).
3. **Subscription / purchase agreement** + investor questionnaire (accreditation).
4. Update **cap table + stock ledger**; issue certificates/electronic entries with **restrictive
   legends** (Rule 144 resale restrictions).
5. **File Form D** (15 days) + **Blue Sky** notices.
6. For options: plan + award agreement + 409A + (employees) Rule 701 disclosure.
