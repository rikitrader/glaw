# corporate-counsel

US corporate lawyer that **drafts all formation & governance documents and keeps any US entity in
compliance** — C-corp, S-corp, LLC, LP/LLP, PBC, nonprofit — in any state, including for foreign
owners. The transactional/formation/compliance counterpart to `elite-corporate-counsel`
(litigation/disputes) and `tax-strategy` (tax/wealth structuring).

## What it does
Picks the entity + state, files the formation documents, drafts the governance set (bylaws /
operating agreement / shareholder + voting agreements / board consents / founder docs), handles
**founder control** (dual-class / super-voting / voting trusts), runs **securities compliance** for
equity issuance (Reg D/506, Rule 701, Form D, Blue Sky, 83(b), 409A), and builds an ongoing
**compliance calendar** (annual report, franchise tax, registered agent, foreign qualification,
**CTA/BOI**, S-corp reasonable comp).

## Scope & limits
Informational drafting — **not legal advice and not a substitute for a licensed attorney** in the
relevant state. State-specific; verify current fees/rules. Every equity issuance needs a securities
exemption + filings. Current-law anchors verified live (June 2026): **CTA/BOI March 2025 interim
rule** (domestic entities exempt; foreign reporting companies only); dual-class facts (Meta ~57%
vote/13.6% equity, SpaceX Class B auto-convert, Tesla/SpaceX→Texas, *Tornetta*); TBOC eff. Sept
2025. Routes disputes to `elite-corporate-counsel`, tax to `tax-strategy`, funds to `pe-vc-counsel`.
