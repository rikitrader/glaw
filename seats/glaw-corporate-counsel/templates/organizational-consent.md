**[COMPANY NAME], INC. — Organizational Consent of the [Incorporator / Board of Directors]**

1. **Certificate of Incorporation.** RESOLVED, the filing of the Certificate with the [State]

   [__], Secretary [__], with the duties in the Bylaws.

   and [#] shares reserved.

8. **EIN & tax.** RESOLVED, the officers obtain an EIN; [the Corporation elect S-corporation status
   on Form 2553 effective [date]]; fiscal year ends [Dec 31].

   restricted-stock purchase.
