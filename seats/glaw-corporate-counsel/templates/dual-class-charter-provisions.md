# DUAL-CLASS STOCK — CERTIFICATE OF INCORPORATION PROVISIONS
*(Drop-in Article for a [Delaware] C-corp; sample — securities/exchange counsel must review before
any public offering. Requires a C-corp; **not** available to S-corps.)*

## ARTICLE [IV] — CAPITAL STOCK

**Section 1. Authorized shares.** The total number of shares the Corporation is authorized to
issue is [____]: [____] shares of **Class A Common** ($[par]), [____] shares of **Class B Common**
($[par]), [optional: [____] shares of **Class C Common (non-voting)**], and [____] shares of
**Preferred** ($[par]), issuable in series by the Board ("blank-check preferred").

**Section 2. Voting.**
(a) **Class A Common — one (1) vote** per share.
(b) **Class B Common — ten (10) votes** per share.
(c) [**Class C Common — no votes**, except as required by law.]
(d) Except as required by law or this Certificate, Class A and Class B **vote together** as a
    single class on all matters.
(e) **Class-protective votes:** the Corporation shall not, without the separate approval of a
    majority of the affected class, [alter the powers/preferences of that class; increase/decrease
    its authorized shares; etc.].

**Section 3. Identical rights otherwise.** Class A, B [and C] are identical as to dividends,
liquidation, and all other matters except voting and conversion.

**Section 4. Conversion of Class B (the control-stays-personal mechanism).**
(a) **Optional:** each Class B share converts to one Class A share at the holder's election.
(b) **Automatic on transfer:** each Class B share **automatically converts to one Class A share
    upon any sale, transfer, assignment, or disposition**, except **Permitted Transfers**
    (to/among: the founder's affiliates, estate-planning vehicles for the founder's benefit,
    spouse/lineal descendants, or entities the founder solely controls).
(c) **Sunset — automatic conversion of ALL Class B to Class A** upon the earliest of: (i) the
    [__]th anniversary of the IPO; (ii) the date the founder (and Permitted Transferees) hold
    fewer than [__]% of the Class B originally issued; or (iii) the founder's death or permanent
    incapacity [+ a transition period of [__] months].
(d) Conversions are automatic, require no further action, and the transfer agent updates the
    ledger accordingly.

**Section 5. No mid-stream increase.** Consistent with stock-exchange voting-rights rules, the
Corporation shall not after [its IPO] issue additional super-voting stock to, or increase the
votes of, existing holders in a manner that disparately reduces other holders' voting rights.

**Section 6. Preferred.** The Board may fix by resolution the designations, powers, preferences,
and rights of each series of Preferred ("blank check").
