# VOTING AGREEMENT
*([State] corporation — sample skeleton; counsel review. Pools votes / board control behind the
Founder. Pair with dual-class charter and an irrevocable proxy where needed.)*

**VOTING AGREEMENT** dated [date] among **[Company], Inc.** (the "Company"), **[Founder]**, and
the stockholders listed on **Schedule A** (each a "Stockholder").

**Recitals.** The parties wish to provide for the voting of their shares as to board composition
and certain matters.

## 1. Board composition
1.1 At each election, each Stockholder shall vote all of its shares to elect:
   (a) [__] directors designated by the **Founder** (so long as the Founder holds ≥[__]%);
   (b) [__] director(s) designated by [investors/Preferred per the financing documents];
   (c) [the CEO]; (d) [__] independent director(s) mutually agreed.
1.2 **Removal/replacement** of a designee only at the request of the party entitled to designate.
1.3 Vacancies filled by the designating party.

## 2. Founder voting control [optional, alongside or instead of dual-class]
2.1 Each Stockholder shall vote (or act by written consent with) all shares **as the Founder
    directs** on [all matters / specified matters], OR
2.2 **grants the Founder an IRREVOCABLE PROXY** to vote its shares, which proxy is **coupled with
    an interest** (the obligations in this Agreement) and is irrevocable to the fullest extent of
    [DGCL §212(e)], until termination under §5.

## 3. Drag-along
3.1 If holders of [__]% (and [the Board / Founder]) approve a **Sale of the Company**, each
    Stockholder shall vote for it, waive appraisal/dissenters' rights, and sell on the same terms
    (subject to customary limits on representations/liability and pro-rata treatment).

## 4. Transfer / legend
4.1 Shares are subject to this Agreement; transferees must join (no transfer otherwise).
4.2 Certificates bear a legend referencing this Agreement.

## 5. Term & termination
5.1 Terminates on the earliest of: a **Sale of the Company**; a **qualified IPO** [or a stated
    post-IPO date]; written consent of [__]%; or [date]. 5.2 Survival of accrued rights.

## 6. General
6.1 Specific performance (damages inadequate). 6.2 Governing law [State]; forum.
6.3 Amendments — written consent of [Company + Founder + holders of __%]. 6.4 Counterparts/
    electronic signatures. 6.5 No partnership/agency beyond voting as stated.
