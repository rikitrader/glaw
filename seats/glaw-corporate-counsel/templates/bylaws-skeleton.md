## ARTICLE I — OFFICES
1.1 Registered office: [agent/address]. 1.2 Other offices as the Board designates.

## ARTICLE II — STOCKHOLDERS
2.1 **Annual meeting** — date/time/place (or by remote communication) fixed by the Board.
2.2 **Special meetings** — called by [Board / Chair / holders of ≥[__]%].
2.3 **Notice** — [10–60] days before the meeting; contents; waiver.
2.4 **Quorum** — holders of a majority of voting power present; adjournment rules.
2.5 **Voting** — one vote per share *[OR per Article — see dual-class charter]*; proxies;
    record date; **action by written consent** [permitted/​prohibited]; inspector of elections.
2.6 **Advance notice** — stockholder proposals/nominations: notice window [90–120] days, contents.

## ARTICLE III — DIRECTORS
3.1 **Powers** — business managed by/under the Board. 3.2 **Number/qualification**.
3.3 **Election/term** — [annual / classified into [3] staggered classes].
3.4 **Vacancies & newly created seats** — filled by [remaining directors].
3.5 **Removal** — [with/without cause] by [holders of __%]. 3.6 **Resignation**.
3.7 **Meetings** — regular/special; notice; **telephonic/electronic**; **written consent** in lieu.
3.8 **Quorum** — majority; act by majority of those present.
3.9 **Committees** — Audit, Compensation, Nominating; authority + charters.
3.10 **Compensation** of directors.
