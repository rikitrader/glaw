## Lessons for Roofing Company Analysis

1. **Roll-up economics:** Acquiring multiple roofing/solar companies via stock creates goodwill that must be justified by EBITDA contribution
2. **Convertible debt trap:** $11.4M in convertible notes = massive dilution risk and interest burden
3. **Related-party transactions:** $1.7M in related-party lease obligations requires scrutiny
4. **Gross margin compression:** 20.4% suggests pricing/cost issues vs. industry 40-55%
5. **Going concern = red flag** for any acquisition target
6. **Stock-based compensation:** $2.67M in non-cash comp inflates overhead
7. **Share dilution:** went from 154M to 249M shares in one year (62% dilution)
