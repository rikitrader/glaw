# Adversarial Protocol — Red Team → Reconstruction → Strategy → Deliverables

## STEP 1 — RED TEAM (destroy the case)
Spin up an independent adversarial unit whose SOLE purpose is to DESTROY the case. It must:
attack every allegation · challenge every assumption · discredit witnesses · expose evidentiary
weaknesses · find logical fallacies · supply alternative innocent explanations · identify missing
evidence · predict defense strategies · simulate opposing counsel.

### Defense Attack Matrix (output, one row per allegation)
| Allegation | Strength (0–100) | Weakness (0–100) | P(dismissal) | P(summary judgment) | P(jury acceptance) | Best defense attack | Alt. innocent explanation | Evidence that would sink us |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Deploy one RED agent per allegation; each tries hardest to KILL its count.

## STEP 2 — RECONSTRUCTION TEAM (rebuild)
After RED, rebuild. For each weakness:
| Weakness | Solution | Evidence needed | Investigation required | Witness required | Expert required |
Repair weaknesses · locate additional evidence · identify missing documents · improve legal
theories · strengthen causation · reinforce damages · develop alternative claims & evidentiary
paths. Keep only counts that survive RED; demote/drop the rest with reasons.

## STEP 3 — LITIGATION INTELLIGENCE
Evaluate per claim: causes of action · jurisdiction · venue · standing · damages · defenses ·
counterclaims. Recommend strongest claims / evidence / witnesses / exhibits.

## STEP 4 — WINNING STRATEGY GENERATOR
- **Strategy A — Most aggressive** litigation path.
- **Strategy B — Most evidence-driven** path.
- **Strategy C — Most settlement-leverage** path.
- **Strategy D — Most cost-effective** path.
- **Strategy E — Worst-case recovery** path.
For each: thesis, sequence of moves, claims used, leverage points, cost/time, expected recovery,
key risks.

## STEP 5 — FINAL DELIVERABLES (produce all)
1. Executive Summary
2. Fraud Findings Report
3. Relationship Map
4. Timeline Analysis
5. Financial Analysis
6. Contradiction Report
7. Red Team Attack Report
8. Reconstruction Report
9. Missing Evidence Report
10. Litigation Readiness Assessment
11. Probability of Success Assessment
12. Recommended Next Investigative Actions
13. Court-Admissibility Assessment
14. Master Case Theory
15. Alternative Case Theories

## CONFIDENCE SCORING (apply everywhere)
Every conclusion gets a **0–100%** score + the reasoning chain. Label each item
**FACT / INFERENCE / HYPOTHESIS / SPECULATION**. Pattern confidence ladder:
Confirmed · Highly Probable · Probable · Possible · Speculative.
Court-Admissibility tag per exhibit: Admissible / Needs authentication / Hearsay-risk / Inadmissible.
