Act of 1934**, the SEC's **procedural rules** (the Rules on Informal and Other Procedures), the
foundational SEC **releases** (the Wells release; the Seaboard Report), and the controlling
**Supreme Court** cases on remedies and limitations. The SEC **Enforcement Manual** is staff
guidance. Every figure (penalty tiers, etc.) defers to `tax-legal-shared/current-figures.md`.

## Case law (real holdings only)

- ***Kokesh v. SEC***, 581 U.S. 455 (2017) — SEC **disgorgement** is a "penalty" subject to the
  5-year § 2462 limitations period. | remedies-cooperation-and-parallel.md
- ***Liu v. SEC***, 591 U.S. 71 (2020) — disgorgement as **equitable relief** is limited to **net
  profits** and generally returned to investors. | remedies-cooperation-and-parallel.md
- ***Gabelli v. SEC***, 568 U.S. 442 (2013) — the § 2462 penalty clock runs from the **violation**,
  not discovery. | the-wells-process-and-submission.md
- ***Lucia v. SEC***, 585 U.S. 237 (2018) — SEC ALJs are "officers" requiring proper appointment.
  | enforcement-lifecycle.md
- ***SEC v. Jarkesy***, 603 U.S. 109 (2024) — Seventh-Amendment jury-trial right for SEC fraud
  claims seeking **civil penalties** (constrains the in-house forum). **Verify the current
  forum landscape**, which is evolving. | enforcement-lifecycle.md
- ***SEC v. W.J. Howey Co.***, 328 U.S. 293 (1946) — the **investment-contract** test (jurisdiction
  argument: is the instrument a security?). | the-wells-process-and-submission.md

> Statutory amendments (e.g., the NDAA 2021 amendments to the Exchange Act on disgorgement) have
> modified the post-*Kokesh*/*Liu* landscape — **confirm the current statute before relying.**
