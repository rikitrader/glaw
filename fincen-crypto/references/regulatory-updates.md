## GENIUS Act — the new frontier (PROPOSED)
- **2026-06-18 — GENIUS Act CIP Joint NPRM** `NPRM` — **Fed. Reg. 2026-12460** (FinCEN +
  OCC/FRB/FDIC/NCUA). Would treat **permitted payment stablecoin issuers** as **BSA financial
  institutions** required to maintain a **CIP** (identity collection/verification,
  recordkeeping, OFAC list comparison). Authority: GENIUS Act; 31 U.S.C. 5318(l); 31 CFR
  1020.220. **Proposed — comment, don't build to final.**
- **2026-04-08 — Treasury proposes GENIUS Act illicit-finance rule** `NPRM` — the broader
  anti-illicit-finance framework for payment stablecoins. **Proposed.**

## Current law (NOT proposed) — already binding on crypto MSBs
- CVC **administrators/exchangers are MSBs** (FinCEN 2013/2019 guidance): must **register**
  (Form 107, 31 CFR 1022.380), keep an **AML program** (1022.210), file **SARs** (1022.320) and
  **CTRs**, and apply the **Travel Rule** (31 CFR 1010.410).
- **OFAC applies fully** to crypto — sanctioned addresses/persons are blocked like fiat.

## Enforcement — crypto is in scope
- **2025-12-09 — Paxful $3.5M** for facilitating suspicious activity with illicit actors
  (AML-program + SAR failures, 31 CFR 1022.210/.320). Earlier corpus: **BitMEX $100M (2021)**;
  **first bitcoin "mixer" penalty (2020)**. Full lessons:
  `../fincen/references/enforcement-actions.md`.

## Related advisories
- **2025-09-08 — Financially motivated sextortion** `Notice` — extortion payments are often
  crypto-denominated; relevant to on-chain tracing of extortion flows.

## Analyst takeaways
