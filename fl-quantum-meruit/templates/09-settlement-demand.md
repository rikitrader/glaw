**Demand.** To resolve this now, remit **$[DEMAND]** on or before **[DEADLINE]**. This
offer is made for settlement purposes (§ 90.408) and expires on the deadline; all rights
are reserved.

Sincerely, [ATTORNEY], Esq. — Fla. Bar [__], Attorney for [PLAINTIFF].
