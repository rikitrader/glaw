## EXPERT REPORT REQUIREMENTS (Rule 26(a)(2)(B))

For retained experts, the report must contain:

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Complete statement of opinions** | All opinions to be expressed |
| **Basis and reasons** | Basis and reasons for each opinion |
| **Facts or data** | Facts or data considered |
| **Exhibits** | Any exhibits to be used |
| **Qualifications** | Qualifications, including publications |
| **Prior testimony** | Cases testified in past 4 years |
| **Compensation** | Statement of compensation |

## DAUBERT CONSIDERATIONS

To ensure admissibility under Daubert v. Merrell Dow Pharmaceuticals:

1. **Reliable methodology**
   - Is the theory testable?
   - Has it been peer reviewed?
   - What is the known error rate?
   - Is it generally accepted?

2. **Reliable application**
   - Did the expert apply the methodology reliably?
   - Is there sufficient factual basis?

3. **Fit**
   - Does the testimony help the trier of fact?
   - Is it relevant to the issues?
