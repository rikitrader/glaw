## Rule 14(a) Impleader Requirements

### Timing
| Period | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| Within 14 days of serving answer | As of right — no leave needed |
| After 14 days | Must obtain leave of court |

### Key Limitations
- Third-party claim must be derivative — "is or may be liable to [defendant] for all or part of the claim against it"
- Cannot use Rule 14 to assert independent claims against a new party
- Third-party defendant may assert defenses to both original and third-party claims

### Jurisdiction
- Supplemental jurisdiction under § 1367 generally applies
- **Exception**: § 1367(b) bars claims by plaintiffs against third-party defendants in diversity cases that would destroy complete diversity
