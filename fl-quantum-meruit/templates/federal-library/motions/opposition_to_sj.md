## Strategic Guidance

### Local Rule 3.01(c) — Counter-Statement of Facts (MANDATORY)
- Must respond to **every** numbered fact in movant's statement
- For each: state "DISPUTED" or "UNDISPUTED"
- For disputed facts: cite specific contradicting evidence with page/line references
- May add "Additional Material Facts" not in movant's statement
- **Failure to respond = facts deemed admitted**

### Key Defense Strategies
1. **Create fact disputes** — even one genuine dispute on a material fact defeats SJ
2. **Challenge materiality** — argue disputed facts actually go to outcome-determinative issues
3. **Credibility** — if resolution depends on believing one witness over another, SJ is improper
4. **Inferences** — insist all inferences favor nonmovant, not movant
5. **Rule 56(d)** — if discovery incomplete, file declaration explaining what's needed

### Record Citations
Every factual assertion must cite specific record evidence:
- Depositions: "Smith Dep. 45:3-12 (Ex. A)"
- Documents: "Email dated 1/15/2025 (Ex. B, Bates 000123)"
- Declarations: "Doe Decl. ¶ 7 (Doc. ___)"
