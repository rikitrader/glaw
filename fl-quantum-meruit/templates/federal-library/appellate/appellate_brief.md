## Eleventh Circuit Brief Requirements

### Word Limits (FRAP 32(a)(7))
| Brief Type | Word Limit |
|------------|------------|
| Opening brief | 13,000 words |
| Response brief | 13,000 words |
| Reply brief | 6,500 words |
| Cross-appeal opening | 13,000 words |

### Formatting Requirements (FRAP 32(a))
- **Typeface**: 14-point proportionally spaced serif font (e.g., Times New Roman)
- **Margins**: At least 1 inch on all sides
- **Line spacing**: Double-spaced (except block quotes and footnotes)
- **Cover**: Blue (appellant), Red (appellee), Gray (reply), Green (amicus)

### Key 11th Circuit Local Rules
| Rule | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| 28-1(a) | Certificate of Interested Persons — **MANDATORY** |
| 28-1(b) | Statement Regarding Oral Argument |
| 28-5 | Record citations use "R." format |
| 28-6 | Unpublished opinions: may cite but not binding |
| 31-1 | Opening brief due 40 days after record designated |
| 31-1 | Response brief due 30 days after opening brief |
| 31-1 | Reply brief due 21 days after response brief |

### Standards of Review — Quick Reference
| Type of Ruling | Standard | Key |
|---------------|----------|-----|
| Summary judgment | De novo | Most favorable to nonmovant |
| Motion to dismiss | De novo | Accept allegations as true |
| Findings of fact (bench trial) | Clear error | Deferential |
| Evidentiary rulings | Abuse of discretion | Deferential |
| Discovery rulings | Abuse of discretion | Very deferential |
| Jury instructions | De novo (legal) | Abuse of discretion (phrasing) |
| Qualified immunity | De novo (legal) | Clear error (factual) |

### Issue Preservation
- Arguments not raised in district court are generally **waived** on appeal
- Exception: plain error review (rare in civil cases)
- Must have obtained a ruling (or objected) to preserve the issue
