## Drafting Notes

### Distinction from TRO
- PI requires notice and hearing (Rule 65(a)); TRO can be ex parte
- PI has no automatic expiration; TRO limited to 14 days
- PI findings of fact and conclusions of law are on the record
- PI is immediately appealable under 28 U.S.C. 1292(a)(1)

### Eleventh Circuit Review
- **Abuse of discretion** standard on appeal
- District court's factual findings reviewed for clear error
- Legal conclusions reviewed de novo
- *Charles H. Wesley Educ. Found., Inc. v. Cox*, 408 F.3d 1349 (11th Cir. 2005)
