## Drafting Notes

### Rule 37(a)(5) — Expenses
- If motion granted: loser pays movant's expenses **unless** substantially justified or other circumstances make award unjust
- If motion denied: movant pays respondent's expenses under same standard
- If granted in part: court may apportion expenses

### Meet-and-Confer Requirement
- Local Rule 3.01(g) requires good faith conference before filing
- Motion must include certification of conference
- Failure to confer = denial of motion

### Proportionality (Rule 26(b)(1))
Court considers: importance of issues, amount in controversy, parties' resources, importance of discovery, whether burden outweighs likely benefit
