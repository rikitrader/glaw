## Drafting Notes

### Winter Factors (Required Showing)
1. **Substantial likelihood of success on the merits**
2. **Irreparable injury** — must be actual and imminent, not speculative
3. **Balance of equities** — harm to plaintiff > harm to defendant
4. **Public interest** — injunction serves or doesn't harm public interest

### Rule 65(b) Requirements
- **14-day limit** (extendable once for good cause) — Rule 65(b)(2)
- **Bond required** unless court finds no risk of harm to defendant
- **Specificity** — order must state reasons for issuance and describe restrained acts in reasonable detail — Rule 65(d)(1)
- **Binding scope** — only parties, officers, agents, and those in active concert with actual notice — Rule 65(d)(2)

### Ex Parte TRO
- Permitted only with "specific facts in an affidavit or verified complaint" showing immediate and irreparable injury — Rule 65(b)(1)
- Must certify in writing efforts to give notice or reasons why notice should not be required
- Court should require hearing within 14 days
