## Drafting Notes

### When to Submit
- File as an attachment to the Motion to Dismiss
- Local Rule 3.01(g) requires a proposed order with every motion

### Key Considerations
- **With vs. Without Prejudice**: With prejudice = no re-filing; without prejudice = leave to amend
- **Leave to Amend**: 11th Circuit strongly favors allowing at least one amendment. *Bryant v. Dupree*, 252 F.3d 1161 (11th Cir. 2001)
- **Partial Dismissal**: If only some counts fail, remaining counts proceed — no case closure
- **12(b)(1) vs. 12(b)(6)**: Jurisdictional dismissal is always without prejudice
