## Drafting Notes

### When to Use
- Extensions of time (most common use — saves motion practice)
- Protective orders for confidential discovery
- Partial settlements / dismissals of individual counts
- Discovery modifications (additional interrogatories, deposition limits)
- Consent to magistrate judge jurisdiction

### Rule 29 — Stipulations
- Parties may stipulate to discovery modifications without court approval unless the modification would interfere with court-set deadlines
- If modifying a Rule 16(b) scheduling order deadline, court approval required

### Practical Tips
- Both counsel must sign before submission
- File as "Joint Motion" or "Stipulation" with proposed order attached
- If extending a court-set deadline, explain "good cause" in the recitals
- Judges appreciate stipulated resolutions — reduces docket burden
