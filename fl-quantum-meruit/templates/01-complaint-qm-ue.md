# COMPLAINT FOR QUANTUM MERUIT AND UNJUST ENRICHMENT

Plaintiff, [PLAINTIFF], sues Defendant, [DEFENDANT], and alleges:

## GENERAL ALLEGATIONS
1. This is an action for damages [exceeding $[AMOUNT]], exclusive of interest, costs,
   and attorney's fees, within the jurisdiction of this Court.
2. Plaintiff is [a Florida resident / [STATE] [entity]] [doing business in [COUNTY]].
3. Defendant is [a Florida resident / [STATE] [entity]] subject to this Court's
   jurisdiction.
4. Venue is proper in [COUNTY] County because [the cause of action accrued here / the
   work was performed here / Defendant resides here].
5. [At Defendant's request, between [DATE] and [DATE], Plaintiff provided the following
   labor, materials, and/or services: [WORK PERFORMED].]
6. [Plaintiff performed the services in a workmanlike manner and Defendant accepted and
   used them.]
7. [The reasonable value of the services is $[VALUE]. Defendant has paid $[PAID],
   leaving $[OUTSTANDING] unpaid.]
8. All conditions precedent to this action have occurred, been performed, or been waived.
9. Plaintiff has retained the undersigned and is obligated to pay a reasonable fee
   [recoverable under [contract / statute], if applicable].

## COUNT I — QUANTUM MERUIT
10. Plaintiff realleges paragraphs 1–9.
11. This count is pleaded [as Plaintiff's primary theory / in the alternative to any
    express contract] in the event the Court determines no enforceable express contract
    governs the parties' dealings.
12. Plaintiff provided labor, materials, and/or services to Defendant at Defendant's
    request.
13. Plaintiff reasonably expected to be compensated, and Defendant knew or should have
    known Plaintiff expected payment.
14. Defendant knowingly accepted, used, and benefited from Plaintiff's services.
15. Defendant has failed to pay the reasonable value of the services, $[OUTSTANDING].
16. It would be inequitable for Defendant to retain the benefit without paying.

**WHEREFORE** Plaintiff demands judgment against Defendant for damages of $[OUTSTANDING],
prejudgment interest, court costs, [attorney's fees if a contractual/statutory basis
exists], and such further relief as the Court deems just.

## COUNT II — UNJUST ENRICHMENT *(pleaded in the alternative to Count I)*
17. Plaintiff realleges paragraphs 1–9.
18. Plaintiff conferred a benefit upon Defendant, namely [WORK / MATERIALS / FUNDS].
19. Defendant had knowledge of the benefit.
20. Defendant voluntarily accepted and retained the benefit.
21. It would be inequitable for Defendant to retain the benefit without paying its value
    of $[OUTSTANDING].

**WHEREFORE** Plaintiff demands judgment against Defendant for the value of the benefit
conferred, $[OUTSTANDING], prejudgment interest, court costs, and such further relief as
the Court deems just.
