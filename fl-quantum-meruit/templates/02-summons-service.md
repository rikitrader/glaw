# A. SUMMONS (clerk issues; one per defendant)

IN THE CIRCUIT COURT OF THE [Nth] JUDICIAL CIRCUIT, IN AND FOR [COUNTY] COUNTY, FLORIDA
CASE NO.: ______________

[PLAINTIFF], Plaintiff, v. [DEFENDANT], Defendant.

**SUMMONS**

THE STATE OF FLORIDA — To Each Sheriff of the State:

YOU ARE COMMANDED to serve this summons and a copy of the Complaint on Defendant:
**[DEFENDANT NAME / REGISTERED AGENT], [SERVICE ADDRESS]**.

Each defendant is required to serve written defenses to the Complaint on Plaintiff's
attorney, **[ATTORNEY NAME, ADDRESS]**, within **20 days** after service of this summons
(exclusive of the day of service), and to file the original with the Clerk either before
service on Plaintiff's attorney or immediately thereafter. **If a defendant fails to do
so, a default will be entered** for the relief demanded in the Complaint.

DATED: __________   CLERK OF THE COURT  By: _______________ Deputy Clerk

# B. SERVICE INSTRUCTIONS
- **Individual:** personal service (§ 48.031) at residence/usual place of abode.
- **Entity (LLC/corp):** serve the **registered agent** (§ 48.062 / § 48.081); confirm the
  current RA on Sunbiz; if RA fails, serve a manager/member or the Secretary of State
  (substitute service) per statute.
- Use a **certified process server** (county sheriff's list) or the Sheriff. Provide:
  summons + Complaint + any civil cover sheet.
- **Deadline:** serve within **120 days** of filing (Rule 1.070(j)) or show good cause.
