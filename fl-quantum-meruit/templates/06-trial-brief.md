## III. LEGAL STANDARD
**Quantum meruit** (Florida): a plaintiff who provides services the defendant requests and
accepts, reasonably expecting payment, may recover their reasonable value where it would
be inequitable for the defendant to retain them unpaid. **Unjust enrichment:** (1) benefit
conferred; (2) defendant's knowledge; (3) acceptance/retention; (4) inequity of retaining
without payment. Both are alternatives to express contract and are barred where a valid
express contract governs the same subject. *Commerce P'ship 8098 v. Equity Contracting*,
695 So. 2d 383 (Fla. 4th DCA 1997); *Maloney v. Therm Alum*, 636 So. 2d 767. **[verify]**
