# COMPLAINT (COMMON COUNTS)
Plaintiff sues Defendant and alleges:
1. This is an action for $[AMOUNT], within the jurisdiction of this Court. [Parties + venue.]

## COUNT I — ACCOUNT STATED
2. Before this action, Plaintiff and Defendant had business transactions and **agreed to the resulting
   balance of $[AMOUNT]**. 3. Plaintiff rendered a statement of account (Ex. A); Defendant **did not
   object** within a reasonable time and **promised** (expressly or by retention) to pay it.
4. Defendant has not paid. **WHEREFORE** Plaintiff demands $[AMOUNT], interest, costs, fees if any.

## COUNT II — OPEN ACCOUNT (alternative)
5. Plaintiff realleges 1. 6. Defendant owes $[AMOUNT] on an **open account** for goods/services
   furnished between [DATE] and [DATE], itemized in the attached statement (Ex. B), verified by
   affidavit. **WHEREFORE** Plaintiff demands $[AMOUNT], interest, costs.

## COUNT III — MONEY LENT (alternative)
7. Plaintiff realleges 1. 8. Plaintiff **lent** Defendant $[AMOUNT] on [DATE], which Defendant agreed
   to repay and has not. **WHEREFORE** Plaintiff demands $[AMOUNT], interest, costs.
