# COMPLAINT (EQUITABLE RELIEF)

## COUNT I — CONSTRUCTIVE TRUST
1. Defendant holds [PROPERTY/funds, traceable] that in equity belongs to Plaintiff because [a promise
   + transfer in reliance within a confidential/fiduciary relationship / fraud / unjust enrichment].
2. It would be inequitable for Defendant to retain it. **WHEREFORE** Plaintiff requests a **constructive
   trust** over [the property] and an order conveying it (and its traceable proceeds) to Plaintiff.

## COUNT II — RESULTING TRUST (alternative)
3. Plaintiff **paid the purchase price** for [PROPERTY]; title was taken in Defendant's name; the
   parties intended Plaintiff to hold the beneficial interest (no gift). **WHEREFORE** Plaintiff
   requests a resulting trust and conveyance of the beneficial interest.

## COUNT III — EQUITABLE ACCOUNTING
4. A [fiduciary relationship / series of complex transactions] exists; the accounts are too
   complicated for a legal remedy; Plaintiff demands an accounting of [the funds/venture/partnership].

   which Plaintiff incurred ($[AMOUNT]). **Common law:** Plaintiff, **wholly without fault**, was held
   liable for Defendant's wrongdoing under a special relationship. **WHEREFORE** Plaintiff demands
   indemnity of $[AMOUNT], costs, and fees.

## COUNT V — EQUITABLE SUBROGATION
6. Plaintiff **paid a debt** ([describe]) to protect its own interest, was **not a volunteer**, and
   the debt was primarily owed by Defendant; the equities favor subrogation. **WHEREFORE** Plaintiff
   requests to be **subrogated** to the creditor's rights against Defendant for $[AMOUNT].
