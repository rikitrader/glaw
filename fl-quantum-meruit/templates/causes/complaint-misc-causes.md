## NEGLIGENT INFLICTION OF EMOTIONAL DISTRESS (SOL 4 yr)
Defendant negligently [ACT]; Plaintiff suffered a **physical impact** [or was in the **zone of danger**
of a foreseeable physical impact and had a **direct personal involvement**]; Plaintiff sustained
emotional distress **with discernible physical manifestation**; damages $[AMOUNT]. *(Florida's impact
rule limits NIED — plead the impact or a recognized exception.)*

## LOSS OF CONSORTIUM (derivative — SOL 4 yr)
**Spousal:** Plaintiff was **married** to [INJURED SPOUSE] when Defendant tortiously injured the
spouse; Plaintiff lost the spouse's **society, services, companionship, and consortium**; damages
$[AMOUNT]. **Filial (§ 768.0415):** Plaintiff's minor child suffered a **significant permanent injury
with permanent total disability**; Plaintiff lost the child's companionship/society. *(Derivative —
fails if the primary claim fails; reduced by the injured person's comparative fault.)*

## BREACH OF JOINT VENTURE AGREEMENT (SOL 4 yr)
The parties formed a **joint venture** — a community of interest, **joint control**, a contribution by
each, and an agreement to **share profits AND losses** — to [VENTURE]. Defendant breached its duties by
[ACT]. Plaintiff was damaged $[AMOUNT] and is entitled to an accounting. *(Distinguish a mere
contract/ employment — sharing of LOSSES + joint control are key.)*

## MISLEADING ADVERTISEMENT (§ 817.41) (SOL 4 yr)
Defendant published an advertisement containing **misleading statements** with **intent that the
public rely**; Plaintiff relied and was injured $[AMOUNT]. *(§ 817.41 supports punitive damages and
fees; reliance is required.)*

## BUILDING CODE VIOLATION (§ 553.84) (SOL 4 yr)
Defendant **violated the Florida Building Code** ([provision]); the provision was intended to protect
persons like Plaintiff; the violation proximately caused Plaintiff's damages $[AMOUNT]. *(§ 553.84 —
limited private right; the "knew or should have known of the violation" exception bars some claims.)*

## STATUTORY / COMMON-LAW WAY OF NECESSITY (§ 704.01)
**Implied (§ 704.01(1)):** at severance from a common grantor, Plaintiff's parcel became **landlocked**;
a way of necessity is implied over the [servient parcel]. **Statutory (§ 704.01(2)):** Plaintiff's land
(used for a dwelling/agriculture/timber/stock) is **shut off from any practicable route to a public
road** except over [defendant's land]; Plaintiff is entitled to a statutory easement on payment of
compensation. **WHEREFORE** Plaintiff requests an easement for ingress/egress and utilities.

## WRONGFUL BIRTH (SOL 2 yr)
Defendant ([medical provider]) **breached a duty** to inform Plaintiffs of a [genetic/developmental]
risk, depriving them of an **informed reproductive choice**; but for the breach Plaintiffs would have
[avoided conception/continuation]; Plaintiffs incurred **extraordinary child-rearing/medical costs** of
$[AMOUNT]. *(Medical → Ch. 766 presuit.)*

**WHEREFORE** (each count) Plaintiff demands compensatory damages, interest, costs, and further relief.
