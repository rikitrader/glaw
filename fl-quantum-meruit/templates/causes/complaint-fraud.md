# COMPLAINT (FRAUD)

Plaintiff sues Defendant and alleges:
1. This is an action for damages exceeding $[AMOUNT], exclusive of interest, costs, and fees.
2. [Parties + jurisdiction + venue + long-arm as applicable.]

## COUNT I — FRAUD / FRAUDULENT [MISREPRESENTATION / INDUCEMENT]
3. On [DATE], at [PLACE/medium], **[SPEAKER for Defendant]** made the following false statement(s) of
   material fact to Plaintiff: **"[QUOTE / DESCRIBE the exact representation]"** (pleaded with
   particularity per Rule 1.120(b)).
4. The statement was **false** when made: in truth, [THE TRUTH].
5. Defendant **knew the statement was false** [or made it with reckless disregard for its truth].
6. Defendant made it **intending to induce** Plaintiff to [act — e.g., enter the contract / pay /
   purchase].
7. Plaintiff **justifiably relied** on the statement and [acted — entered the contract / paid $[AMOUNT]].
8. As a direct and proximate result, Plaintiff suffered damages of **$[AMOUNT]**.
   [Fraudulent inducement: the fraud induced [the contract] and is **independent** of any breach
   (the independent-tort exception to the economic-loss rule — *HTP v. Lineas Aereas*).]

**WHEREFORE** Plaintiff demands judgment for compensatory damages of $[AMOUNT], [rescission as an
alternative,] prejudgment interest, costs, and such further relief as is just. [Plaintiff will move
under § 768.72 for leave to add punitive damages upon a reasonable evidentiary proffer.]

## COUNT II — NEGLIGENT MISREPRESENTATION (alternative)
9. Plaintiff realleges 1–2, 3, 7. 10. Defendant made the material representation **without reasonable
   care** as to its truth, intending Plaintiff to rely; Plaintiff justifiably relied and was damaged
   $[AMOUNT].

**WHEREFORE** Plaintiff demands judgment of $[AMOUNT], interest, costs, and further relief.
