# MOTION FOR PROCEEDINGS SUPPLEMENTARY AND TO IMPLEAD THIRD PARTIES (§ 56.29)

Judgment Creditor [CREDITOR] moves under **§ 56.29, Fla. Stat.**, for proceedings supplementary to
execution, to implead the third party named below, and for issuance of a Notice to Appear, and states:

1. On [DATE] this Court entered a final money judgment in favor of [CREDITOR] and against [DEBTOR]
   for $[AMOUNT], recorded at [BOOK/PAGE / OR docket entry __]. A certified copy is Exhibit A.
2. The judgment is valid and outstanding, and **$[UNSATISFIED] remains unsatisfied**.
3. A writ of execution [issued on [DATE] / is being issued] and is valid and outstanding (Exhibit B).
4. **Impleader:** [THIRD PARTY] holds property of the Judgment Debtor and/or owes the Judgment Debtor
   a debt, and/or received a transfer of the Judgment Debtor's property without fair consideration,
   described with reasonable particularity as: [DESCRIBE — e.g., funds in account no. [___] at [BANK];
   the [VEHICLE/REAL PROPERTY]; the $[AMOUNT] transferred on [DATE] to [TRANSFEREE]].
5. The transfer(s) to [THIRD PARTY] are voidable under Ch. 726 (FUFTA) because [made without
   reasonably equivalent value while the Debtor was insolvent / made to an insider / made with intent
   to hinder, delay, or defraud creditors]. [Plead the badges of fraud present.]

**WHEREFORE** Judgment Creditor requests that the Court: (a) commence proceedings supplementary;
(b) issue a **Notice to Appear** to [THIRD PARTY] under § 56.29 directing it to file a responsive
affidavit by a date certain **not less than 7 business days** from service, describing the property
above; (c) after hearing, **levy upon and apply** the Judgment Debtor's non-exempt property — and any
property, debt, or obligation owed — toward the judgment; (d) enter a **money judgment against the
transferee** to the extent of the voidable transfer; (e) award costs and reasonable attorney's fees
under § 56.29; and (f) grant such further relief as is just.
