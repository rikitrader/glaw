## [PROPOSED] WRIT OF EXECUTION
THE STATE OF FLORIDA TO EACH SHERIFF OF THE STATE: You are commanded to levy upon the goods, chattels,
and non-exempt real and personal property of [DEBTOR] (§ 56.061) sufficient to satisfy $[UNSATISFIED]

## SUPPORT — Fact Information Sheet (Rule 1.560) + Judgment Lien
