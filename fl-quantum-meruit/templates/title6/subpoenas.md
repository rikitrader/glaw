# SHARED CAPTION
```
IN THE CIRCUIT COURT OF THE [Nth] JUDICIAL CIRCUIT IN AND FOR [COUNTY] COUNTY, FLORIDA
[PLAINTIFF], Plaintiff, v. [DEFENDANT], Defendant.        CASE NO.: __________
```

## FORM 1 — SUBPOENA FOR TRIAL/HEARING (Rule 1.410(b))
THE STATE OF FLORIDA TO: [WITNESS NAME, ADDRESS].
YOU ARE COMMANDED to appear before the Honorable [JUDGE] at [COURTHOUSE ADDRESS], Courtroom [__],
on [DATE] at [TIME] to testify in this action. If you fail to appear you may be held in contempt.
You are subpoenaed by [PARTY] through the undersigned attorney, who will be responsible for costs
and witness fees. [Witness fee $[__] + mileage tendered per § 92.142.]
DATED [DATE]. _________________ Clerk / [ATTORNEY], Fla. Bar No. [__], as officer of the court.

## FORM 2 — SUBPOENA FOR DEPOSITION (Rule 1.410(b); served with a Rule 1.310 notice)
THE STATE OF FLORIDA TO: [DEPONENT NAME, ADDRESS].
YOU ARE COMMANDED to appear at [LOCATION / remote platform] on [DATE] at [TIME] to testify at a
deposition upon oral examination in this action, and to remain until discharged. [If an organization,
Rule 1.310(b)(6): the matters for examination are [LIST]; designate one or more persons to testify.]
DATED [DATE]. _________________ [ATTORNEY], Fla. Bar No. [__], as officer of the court.

## FORM 3 — SUBPOENA DUCES TECUM TO A PARTY-WITNESS (Rule 1.410(c))
THE STATE OF FLORIDA TO: [WITNESS NAME, ADDRESS].
YOU ARE COMMANDED to appear at [LOCATION] on [DATE] at [TIME] [for deposition/hearing] AND to bring
the following documents and things: 
1. [Document category 1]. 2. [Document category 2]. 3. [Document category 3].
You may comply by delivering legible copies to [ATTORNEY] before that date in lieu of appearance, by
agreement. Objections must be served within the time allowed by Rule 1.410(c).
DATED [DATE]. _________________ [ATTORNEY], Fla. Bar No. [__], as officer of the court.

## FORM 4 — SUBPOENA DUCES TECUM TO A NON-PARTY (Rule 1.351 + Rule 1.410(c))
**PREREQUISITE:** before issuing, serve all parties with a **Rule 1.351 Notice of Production From
Non-Party** describing the documents and identifying the non-party, and wait **10 days** for
objection. If no objection, then issue:
THE STATE OF FLORIDA TO: [NON-PARTY CUSTODIAN — e.g., [BANK] Records Custodian, ADDRESS].
YOU ARE COMMANDED to produce, on or before [DATE], at [ATTORNEY ADDRESS] (or by certified copies by
mail/email), the following records concerning [SUBJECT — e.g., account no. [____], [DEBTOR/PARTY]]:
1. [All account statements from [DATE] to [DATE]]. 2. [Signature cards / account-opening records].
3. [Wire/ACH/check records]. 4. [Loan/security documents].
A custodian's certificate of authenticity (§ 90.902(11)) is requested in lieu of personal appearance.
DATED [DATE]. _________________ [ATTORNEY], Fla. Bar No. [__], as officer of the court.
