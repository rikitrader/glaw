# A. PETITION FOR WRIT OF QUO WARRANTO (Ch. 80)
Use: challenge a person's right to **hold a public office** or to **exercise a franchise/privilege**,
or a public officer's exercise of power beyond authority. Typically brought by the State (Attorney
General / state attorney); a private relator must show a special injury or interest.
```
[CAPTION] STATE OF FLORIDA ex rel. [RELATOR], Petitioner, v. [RESPONDENT], Respondent. CASE NO. ____
PETITION FOR WRIT OF QUO WARRANTO
1. This Court has jurisdiction to issue writs of quo warranto.
2. Respondent [holds / exercises] the office/franchise of [OFFICE/FRANCHISE].
3. Respondent has no lawful authority to do so because [BASIS — e.g., ineligibility, invalid
   appointment, ultra vires exercise of power].
WHEREFORE Petitioner requests a writ of quo warranto requiring Respondent to show by what authority
[he/she/it] holds the office/exercises the power, and ousting Respondent if the authority is wanting.
```

# B. PETITION FOR WRIT OF PROHIBITION (Ch. 81)
Use: forbid a **lower tribunal** from acting **in excess of its jurisdiction** where there is no
other adequate remedy. Directed to the tribunal (and the opposing party as a nominal respondent).
```
[CAPTION] [PETITIONER], Petitioner, v. THE HON. [JUDGE] / [LOWER TRIBUNAL], Respondent. CASE NO. ____
PETITION FOR WRIT OF PROHIBITION
1. In the proceeding below ([COURT], Case No. [____]), the tribunal is about to act on [MATTER].
2. The tribunal lacks jurisdiction to do so because [BASIS — e.g., disqualification denied, no
   subject-matter jurisdiction, an act beyond its judicial power].
3. Petitioner has no other adequate remedy.
WHEREFORE Petitioner requests a writ of prohibition barring the tribunal from [the act], and a stay.
```

# C. PETITION FOR WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS (Ch. 79)
Use: test the **legality of a person's detention/restraint**.
```
[CAPTION] [PETITIONER], on behalf of [DETAINEE], Petitioner, v. [CUSTODIAN], Respondent. CASE NO. ____
PETITION FOR WRIT OF HABEAS CORPUS
1. [DETAINEE] is restrained of liberty by [CUSTODIAN] at [PLACE] since [DATE].
2. The restraint is unlawful because [BASIS]. 3. No prior application has been made [or state prior
   applications]. Sworn under penalties of perjury. ___________ [PETITIONER].
WHEREFORE Petitioner requests issuance of the writ and the detainee's discharge / lawful disposition.
```
