# COMPLAINT FOR UNLAWFUL DETAINER

Plaintiff sues Defendant and alleges:
1. This is an action for unlawful detainer of real property in [COUNTY] County, governed by the
   summary procedure of § 51.011 (§ 82.04).
2. Plaintiff is entitled to possession of the premises at [ADDRESS] as [owner / lawful possessor]
   (Exhibit A).
3. Defendant entered or remained in possession **as a [guest / invitee / licensee] without any lease,
   rental agreement, or landlord-tenant relationship, and without payment of rent**.
4. Plaintiff revoked any permission and demanded that Defendant vacate on [DATE] (Exhibit B), but
   Defendant **refuses to surrender possession** and unlawfully detains the premises.
5. There is **no landlord-tenant relationship** and Defendant asserts no claim of title or ownership.

**WHEREFORE** Plaintiff demands judgment for **possession**, a writ of possession, **double damages**
for the period of unlawful detention (§ 82.091), costs, and such further relief as is just.
