# COMPLAINT FOR DAMAGES — DISHONORED CHECK (§ 68.065)

Plaintiff sues Defendant and alleges:
1. This is an action under § 68.065, Fla. Stat., for damages arising from a dishonored payment
   instrument, within the jurisdiction of this Court.
2. On [DATE], Defendant delivered to Plaintiff check no. [____] drawn on [BANK], account [____], in
   the amount of **$[AMOUNT]**, in payment for [GOODS/SERVICES] (Exhibit A).
3. The check was **dishonored** by the drawee for [insufficient funds / account closed / no account /
   stop payment without cause], as shown by [the bank's notation / return record] (Exhibit B).
4. On [DATE], Plaintiff served on Defendant, by certified mail [or as allowed], the **written demand
   for payment** required by § 68.065(3), in the statutory form (Exhibit C).

service charges; (d) court costs and reasonable attorney's fees; and such further relief as is just.
