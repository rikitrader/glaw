## Bank Secrecy Act (Title 31) — FBAR

- **31 U.S.C. §5314; 31 C.F.R. §1010.350** — FBAR (FinCEN Form 114) obligation.
- **31 U.S.C. §5321 / §5322** — civil and criminal FBAR penalties (willful penalty + criminal).

## Forms

FinCEN 114 (FBAR), 8938, 5471, 5472, 8865, 8858, 8833 (treaty position), 8840 (closer connection),
8843, 8288 / 8288-A (FIRPTA), 1042 / 1042-S (Chapter 3/4 reporting), W-8BEN / W-8BEN-E / W-8ECI,
W-9, and the Streamlined certification forms (14653 foreign / 14654 domestic).

## Case law (real holdings only)

- ***Bittner v. United States***, 143 S. Ct. 713 (2023) — the **non-willful** FBAR penalty is
  assessed **per report (per year), not per account**.
- ***United States v. Boyle***, 469 U.S. 241 (1985) — reliance on an agent to file is generally not
  reasonable cause (relevant to late-filing penalty defenses).

## OECD / international overlay

- **OECD GloBE / Pillar Two** — 15% global minimum tax for MNE groups above the revenue threshold.
- **OECD Common Reporting Standard (CRS)** — automatic exchange of account information among
  participating jurisdictions (the FATCA reciprocal).
