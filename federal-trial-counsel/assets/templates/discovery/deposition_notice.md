## DEPOSITION LIMITS (FRCP 30)

| Aspect | Limit |
|--------|-------|
| Number of depositions | 10 per side (absent stipulation or leave) |
| Duration | 1 day of 7 hours |
| Re-deposition | Not permitted without leave |
| Notice | Reasonable notice required |

## OBJECTIONS AT DEPOSITION

| Objection Type | When to Make |
|----------------|--------------|
| Form of question | Must be made at deposition (waived if not) |
| Privilege | Must be made at deposition |
| Relevance | Reserved for trial (need not object) |
| Foundation | May be made or reserved |
