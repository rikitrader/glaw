## Drafting Notes

### Rule 11 — Safe Harbor
- Must serve motion on opposing party 21 days before filing with court
- Opposing party has 21 days to withdraw or correct the offending paper
- Sanctions are discretionary, not mandatory

### Rule 37(b)(2) — Discovery Sanctions Menu
Escalating: (i) establish facts, (ii) prohibit evidence, (iii) strike pleadings, (iv) stay proceedings, (v) dismiss/default, (vi) contempt

### 28 U.S.C. § 1927 — Attorney Liability
- Applies only to attorneys (not parties)
- Requires "unreasonably and vexatiously" multiplied proceedings
- *Schwartz v. Millon Air, Inc.*, 341 F.3d 1220 (11th Cir. 2003)

### Inherent Power
- Requires finding of bad faith — *Barnes v. Dalton*, 158 F.3d 1212 (11th Cir. 1998)
- Most severe sanctions require clear and convincing evidence
