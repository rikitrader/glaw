## Drafting Notes

### Rule 16(b) Requirements
- Must be issued "as soon as practicable" after Rule 26(f) conference
- Must limit time to: join parties, amend pleadings, complete discovery, file motions
- May only be modified for "good cause" — *Sosa v. Airprint Systems, Inc.*, 133 F.3d 1417 (11th Cir. 1998)

### MDFL Typical Timeline
| Phase | Typical Timeframe |
|-------|-------------------|
| Initial Disclosures | 14 days after Rule 26(f) conference |
| Amendment of Pleadings | 60-90 days |
| Fact Discovery | 6-9 months |
| Expert Disclosures | 30-60 days after close of fact discovery |
| Dispositive Motions | 30 days after close of all discovery |
| Mediation | Before dispositive motion deadline |
| Trial | 12-18 months from filing |

### Case Management Report (Local Rule 3.05)
Parties must jointly file report addressing: nature of action, basis for jurisdiction, claims/defenses, discovery plan, ADR, trial estimate
