## Drafting Notes

### Local Rule 3.01(c) Compliance
- Statement of Undisputed Material Facts is **mandatory** in MDFL
- Each fact must be supported by citation to record evidence
- Opposing party must file counter-statement identifying disputed facts

### Key Standards
- **Anderson v. Liberty Lobby**, 477 U.S. 242 (1986) — materiality standard
- **Celotex Corp. v. Catrett**, 477 U.S. 317 (1986) — burden shifting
- **Matsushita Elec. v. Zenith Radio**, 475 U.S. 574 (1986) — more than metaphysical doubt

### Partial Summary Judgment
- Rule 56(a) permits summary judgment on "part of each claim or defense"
- Rule 56(g) allows establishing facts as undisputed even when full SJ denied
- Narrows issues for trial and can be strategically valuable
