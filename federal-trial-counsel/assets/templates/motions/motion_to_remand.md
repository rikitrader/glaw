## Key Remand Rules

### 30-Day Deadline (Procedural Defects Only)
- Motion based on procedural defects must be filed within 30 days of removal
- Jurisdictional defects (no SMJ) can be raised **at any time** — 28 U.S.C. § 1447(c)

### Removal Burden
- Removing party bears **burden of proving** federal jurisdiction
- All ambiguities resolved in favor of remand — *Burns v. Windsor Ins. Co.*, 31 F.3d 1092, 1095 (11th Cir. 1994)

### Common Remand Grounds

| Ground | Statute | Deadline |
|--------|---------|----------|
| No diversity | § 1332 | Any time |
| Amount in controversy | § 1332 | Any time |
| No federal question | § 1331 | Any time |
| Forum defendant | § 1441(b)(2) | 30 days |
| Unanimity (not all defendants consent) | § 1446(b)(2)(A) | 30 days |
| Untimely removal | § 1446(b)(1) | 30 days |
| One-year bar | § 1446(c)(1) | 30 days |
