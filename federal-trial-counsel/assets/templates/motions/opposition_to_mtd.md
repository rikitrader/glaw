## Strategic Guidance

### Response Framework
1. **Never concede elements** — argue each element is adequately pled
2. **Pin to specific paragraphs** — cite exact Complaint paragraphs for each element
3. **Lower the bar** — emphasize plausibility is "not a high threshold"
4. **Distinguish cases** — defendant will cite cases where facts were thinner
5. **Always request leave to amend** — preserve right to cure any deficiency

### Key 11th Circuit Cases for Opposition
| Issue | Case | Holding |
|-------|------|---------|
| Low threshold | *Quality Auto Painting*, 917 F.3d 1249 | "not high" standard |
| Accept facts as true | *Dusek*, 832 F.3d 1243 | Light most favorable to plaintiff |
| Leave to amend | *Bryant v. Dupree*, 252 F.3d 1161 | Freely given |
| Pro se leniency | *Tannenbaum v. United States*, 148 F.3d 1262 | Liberal construction |
