**1. EVENTS.**
**(a) Equity Financing.** On an Equity Financing (a bona fide priced preferred round) before this SAFE terminates,
the SAFE automatically converts into the number of shares of **SAFE Preferred Stock** equal to the Purchase Amount
÷ the **Conversion Price** (the lower of (i) the Safe Price [Post-Money Valuation Cap ÷ the Company Capitalization]
and (ii) the Discount Price [the round price × the Discount Rate]).
**(b) Liquidity Event** (Change of Control / IPO / direct listing). The Investor receives, at its option, the
**Cash-Out Amount** (the Purchase Amount) or the number of shares of Common Stock = Purchase Amount ÷ the
Liquidity Price (Post-Money Valuation Cap ÷ Liquidity Capitalization). Cash-Out payments rank junior to debt/other
SAFEs share pro-rata if funds are insufficient.
**(c) Dissolution Event.** The Investor is paid the Purchase Amount (junior to creditors, pari passu with other SAFEs
and preferred) before any distribution to common.
**(d) Termination.** The SAFE terminates on the conversion/payment under (a)–(c).
