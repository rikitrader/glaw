# WRITTEN CONSENT OF SHAREHOLDERS IN LIEU OF MEETING OF [COMPANY], INC. (A FLORIDA CORPORATION)

The undersigned, being shareholders (the "**Shareholders**") of [COMPANY], INC., a Florida corporation ("**[COMPANY]**"), holding no less than a majority in the aggregate of issued and outstanding capital stock of [COMPANY], hereby waive all requirements as to notice of meeting and hereby consent and agree to the adoption of the resolutions set forth below in lieu of taking such action at a formal special meeting, pursuant to Section 607.0704, Florida Statutes:

**WHEREAS**, the Board of Directors of [COMPANY] has determined that it is advisable, fair to and in the best interests of [COMPANY] and its Shareholders to effect a merger upon the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in the Merger Agreement pursuant to which [ACQUISITION ENTITY] will merge with and into [COMPANY] and [COMPANY] shall continue as the surviving corporation (the "**Merger**"), and each outstanding share of common stock, par value $[PAR VALUE] per share, of [COMPANY], each outstanding share of Series A Preferred Stock, par value $[PAR VALUE] per share, of [COMPANY] and each outstanding share of Series B Preferred Stock, par value $[PAR VALUE] per share, of [COMPANY] shall be converted into the right to receive cash as set forth in the Merger Agreement, all upon the terms and subject to the conditions of the Agreement and Plan of Merger by and Among [PARENT ENTITY], [ACQUISITION ENTITY] and [COMPANY] dated [DATE] (the "**Merger Agreement**") attached hereto as Exhibit A;

**WHEREAS**, the Board of Directors of [COMPANY] has recommended to the Shareholders that they approve and adopt the Merger Agreement and the plan of merger contained in the Merger Agreement; and

**WHEREAS**, the Shareholders desire to vote all shares of [COMPANY] capital stock owned by each of them or for which they have the right to vote in favor of the adoption of the Merger Agreement, and the approval of the Merger and the other transactions contemplated by the Merger Agreement.

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED**, that the Merger Agreement and the plan of merger contained in the Merger Agreement is hereby authorized, approved and adopted, and the terms and conditions of each of the transactions contemplated thereby are hereby approved;

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that any and all actions heretofore taken by the officers of [COMPANY] within the terms of the foregoing resolution are hereby ratified, approved and confirmed, and declared to be the valid and binding acts and deeds of [COMPANY]; and

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the officers of [COMPANY] be and they hereby are authorized, directed and empowered to do all such other acts and things and to execute and deliver all such certificates or other documents and to take such other action as they deem necessary or desirable to carry out the purposes and intent of the above resolutions.

**IN WITNESS WHEREOF**, the undersigned, being Shareholders of [COMPANY], hereby execute this Written Consent of Shareholders in Lieu of Meeting, which shall be effective upon the execution of the Merger Agreement (provided, however, this consent shall expire and be of no further force and effect automatically upon any amendment or modification to, or waiver under, the Merger Agreement, but shall otherwise be irrevocable), with respect to the Shares owned by them or which they have the right to vote in favor of the adoption of the Merger Agreement, which number of shares is specified below their signature on the relevant signature page of this consent, and shall have the same force and effect as a Shareholder vote at a duly called meeting of the Shareholders and shall be filed with the minutes of proceedings of the Shareholders in the corporate records.
