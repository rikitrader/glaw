> **Florida note on takeover / anti-takeover statutes.** Florida has **no direct equivalent of DGCL §203**
> (business-combination moratorium). The Florida analogues — applicable, if at all, only to corporations with the
> statutorily required shareholder base — are the **affiliated transactions statute, §607.0901, Fla. Stat.**
> (supermajority/disinterested-shareholder approval for transactions with an interested shareholder) and the
> **control-share acquisitions statute, §607.0902, Fla. Stat.** (voting rights of control shares conditioned on
> disinterested-shareholder approval). Confirm whether either applies and whether the articles/bylaws opt out.
> Any merger structure is governed by **§607.1101 et seq.**, and appraisal/dissenters' rights arise under
> **§§607.1301–607.1340**.
