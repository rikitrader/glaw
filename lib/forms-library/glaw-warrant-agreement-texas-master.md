## 1. CORPORATE AUTHORITY; AUTHORIZATION & RESERVATION OF SHARES
**1.1 Rights and options (TBOC §21.207).** The Company's board of directors has, under **Tex. Bus. Orgs. Code §21.207** (rights and options to acquire shares), the power to create and issue these warrants as **rights or options** entitling the holders to purchase shares of the Company, in the amounts, at the prices, and on the terms and conditions fixed by the board and set out in this Agreement. The board has duly authorized the creation, issuance, and terms of the Warrants and the issuance of the shares issuable on exercise (the "Warrant Shares").
**1.2 Consideration & issuance (TBOC §21.160–.161).** The Warrant Shares are to be issued for consideration fixed by the board that is **adequate** under **TBOC §21.160** (consideration for shares) and, when issued for the consideration so fixed, will be **fully paid and nonassessable** under **TBOC §21.161** (issuance of shares deemed fully paid and nonassessable).
**1.3 Reservation.** The Company shall at all times **reserve and keep available**, free of preemptive rights and other liens, out of its authorized but unissued shares, the full number of Warrant Shares. The Company's certificate of formation authorizes a number of shares of the class issuable on exercise sufficient to cover all outstanding Warrants on a fully-diluted basis.
**1.4 No preemptive rights (TBOC §21.203).** Under **Tex. Bus. Orgs. Code §21.203**, a shareholder **does not have a preemptive right** to acquire the Company's unissued or treasury shares **except to the extent the certificate of formation expressly provides**; the issuance of the Warrants and the Warrant Shares is therefore free of statutory preemptive rights unless and to the extent the certificate of formation grants them, in which case such rights have been waived or satisfied.
