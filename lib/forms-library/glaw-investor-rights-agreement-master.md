## ARTICLE II — REGISTRATION RIGHTS
**2.1 Mandatory / Demand Registration.** On the Investors' request (after the IPO or per the threshold), the Company
files and keeps effective a registration statement covering the Registrable Securities; [demand + S-3 + piggyback]
mechanics. **2.2 Allowed Delay.** The Company may suspend for a limited period for a material development (blackout).
**2.3 Expenses.** The Company pays registration expenses (ex-selling commissions). **2.4 Company Obligations.**
Prepare/file, respond to the SEC, deliver prospectuses, listing, blue-sky, keep effective. **2.5 Due Diligence /
Information.** Investor access for diligence subject to confidentiality. **2.6 Obligations of the Investors.**
Furnish required information; comply with the plan of distribution; lock-up cooperation. **2.7 Indemnification.**
Company indemnifies Investors (and vice-versa for Investor-supplied info); contribution fallback. **2.8 Applicability.**
Article II applies for as long as the Investors hold Registrable Securities (or until Rule 144 freely available).

## ARTICLE III — OTHER RIGHTS
**3.1 Right of First Offer (ROFO).** Before issuing New Securities, the Company first offers the Investors their
pro-rata share on the same terms (with an over-allotment). **3.2 Due Diligence in Subsequent Financings.**
**3.3 Preemptive Rights.** Pro-rata participation in New Securities (carve-outs = Excluded Securities: option-pool
grants, M&A, etc.). **3.4 Additional Rights / MFN.** If the Company grants better rights to a later investor, the
Investors get them. **3.5 Voting.** Voting/drag/voting-agreement undertakings as agreed. **3.6 Transfers.** Transfer
restrictions; permitted transferees take subject to the Agreement; ROFR/co-sale cross-reference. **3.7 Standstill.**
The Investors will not, for [_] years, acquire >[__]% or launch a proxy/tender without Board consent (with fall-aways).

## ARTICLE IV — NOMINATION OF INVESTOR DIRECTORS
**4.1 Investor Directors.** So long as the Sponsor Group holds ≥ the Board Threshold, it may designate [N] directors.
**4.2 Continuing Designation.** Re-nomination, vacancy fill, removal at the Investor's direction. **4.3 Committees.**
Proportionate committee representation [as permitted by law/exchange]. **4.4 Independence/Qualification.** **4.5
Voting Support.** The Company/others support the designees' election. **4.6 Fees and Expenses.** Reimburse director
expenses; same compensation as other non-employee directors. **4.7 Board Observer.** Below the director threshold,
a non-voting observer (subject to confidentiality/privilege carve-outs). **4.8 D&O Indemnification.** **4.9 Reporting
Information.** **4.10 Directors and Officers Insurance** ([$_] tail/coverage).

## ARTICLE V — CONSENT RIGHTS (PROTECTIVE PROVISIONS)
**5.1 Investor Approval.** So long as the threshold is met, the Company will not (without Investor consent): amend
the charter/bylaws adverse to the Investors; create senior/pari securities; pay dividends; redeem/repurchase;
incur debt over [$_]; sell/encumber material assets or the company; change the authorized board size; change the
principal business; related-party transactions; liquidate/dissolve; or other enumerated major actions.

monthly [if held above threshold]; annual budget. **6.2 Reasonable Information / Inspection.** Books-and-records
access on reasonable notice, subject to confidentiality.
