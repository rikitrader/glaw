## SPECIAL STATE LEGEND — FLORIDA RESIDENTS

**FOR FLORIDA RESIDENTS ONLY.** The Shares referred to herein will be sold to, and acquired by, the purchaser in a transaction exempt under **Section 517.061(11) of the Florida Securities and Investor Protection Act (Fla. Stat. Ch. 517)**. The Shares have not been registered under said Act in the State of Florida.

**THREE-DAY RIGHT OF RESCISSION.** Pursuant to **Section 517.061(11)(a)(5), Fla. Stat.**, where sales are made to five (5) or more persons in Florida, any sale made in Florida is voidable by the Florida purchaser **within three (3) days** after the first tender of consideration is made by such purchaser to the Company, an agent of the Company, or an escrow agent, or within three (3) days after the availability of that privilege is communicated to such purchaser, whichever occurs later. To exercise this privilege a Florida subscriber need only send a letter or telegram to the Company at the address set forth in this Memorandum, indicating the subscriber's intention to withdraw, within the three-day period. Such letter or telegram should be sent and postmarked prior to the end of the third business day. If a Florida subscriber sends a letter, it is prudent to send it by certified mail, return receipt requested, to ensure that it is received and to evidence the time of mailing.

## SUITABILITY STANDARDS

An investment in our common stock is suitable only for persons who have sufficient financial means to afford a total loss of their investment and who also have no need for liquidity. These standards have been imposed to enable us to comply with our obligations under applicable federal and state securities laws. These suitability standards are minimum requirements.

The Company must reasonably believe that each investor has sufficient financial means to afford a total loss of the investment and, either alone or with a purchaser representative, has such knowledge and experience in financial and business matters that the investor is capable of adequately evaluating the merits and risks of the investment. Each investor must acquire the Shares for the investor's own account, for investment purposes only, and not with a view to resale or distribution.

The Shares may be sold to an unlimited number of "**accredited investors**" as defined in **Rule 501 of Regulation D**, and to no more than **35 non-accredited investors**.

For purposes hereof, an "**accredited investor**" means any person who comes within (or whom we reasonably believe comes within) any of the categories defined in **Rule 501(a)** of Regulation D at the time of sale, including, among others:

- (i) banks, savings-and-loan associations, registered broker-dealers, insurance companies, registered investment companies, business development companies, SBICs, and qualifying employee-benefit plans;
- (ii) any private business development company as defined in Section 202(a)(22) of the Investment Advisers Act of 1940;
- (iii) any 501(c)(3) organization, corporation, business trust, or partnership not formed to acquire the securities, with total assets in excess of $5,000,000;
- (iv) any director, executive officer, or general partner of the issuer;
- (v) any natural person whose individual net worth, or joint net worth with that person's spouse, exceeds $1,000,000 (excluding primary residence);
- (vi) any natural person with individual income over $200,000 (or $300,000 jointly with spouse) in each of the two most recent years with a reasonable expectation of the same in the current year;
- (vii) any trust with total assets in excess of $5,000,000 not formed to acquire the securities and directed by a sophisticated person; and
- (viii) any entity in which all equity owners are accredited investors.

Prior to acceptance of any subscription, each prospective investor must represent, by completing and signing the Subscription Agreement, that (among other things): the investor understands the Shares are a speculative, high-risk, illiquid investment that has not been registered under the Act or applicable state blue-sky laws; the Shares will bear a restrictive legend; the investor is acquiring the Shares for investment for its own account; the investor has been afforded the opportunity to ask questions and receive answers; the investor has substantial knowledge and experience in business and financial matters; and the investor is able to bear the economic risk of an illiquid investment, including total loss.
