# [COMPANY], INC., a Texas corporation — [20__] EQUITY INCENTIVE PLAN

**1. PURPOSE.** To attract, retain, and motivate eligible persons by offering Awards tied to the Company's future
performance: Options (ISOs/NQSOs), Restricted Stock Awards (RSAs), Stock Bonus Awards, Stock Appreciation Rights
(SARs), Restricted Stock Units (RSUs), and Performance Awards. Defined terms are in Section 29.

**2. SHARES SUBJECT TO THE PLAN.** The number of Shares reserved is **[# / formula]** (plus any evergreen/rollover
Shares as specified), subject to adjustment (Section 20). Sub-limits: maximum Shares issuable as **ISOs = [#]**.
Shares from forfeited/withheld/net-settled Awards return to the pool as the Plan provides. Source: authorized but
unissued or reacquired (treasury) Shares of the Company's common stock. The board's authority to issue Shares under
this Plan, and to fix the consideration for them, is exercised under the **Texas Business Organizations Code, Chapter
21** — **§21.160** (a corporation may issue shares for any tangible or intangible benefit to the corporation,
including cash, promissory notes, services performed, contracts for services to be performed, or other securities of
the corporation) and **§21.161** (before the shares are issued the board must determine that the consideration is
adequate; that determination by the board is conclusive as to the adequacy of consideration and the shares are
thereupon fully paid and nonassessable). The aggregate number of Shares reserved under this Plan may not exceed the
shares authorized in the Company's Certificate of Formation.

**3. ELIGIBILITY.** Awards may be granted to employees, officers, directors, consultants, and advisors of the
Company and its Parents/Subsidiaries/Affiliates; **ISOs only to employees** of the Company or a Parent/Subsidiary,
subject to §422.

**4. ADMINISTRATION.** The Committee (the board or a delegated committee, "Administrator") has full authority to
select grantees, set terms, interpret the Plan, adopt rules, and make all determinations (final and binding);
delegation to a committee of one or more directors as permitted by **§21.416, Texas Business Organizations Code**;
Rule 16b-3 / exchange-rule compliance for officer/director grants.

**5. OPTIONS.** Grant of ISOs/NQSOs. **Exercise price ≥ 100% of FMV** on the grant date (**110%** for an ISO to a
Greater-Than-10% Stockholder). Term ≤ **10 years** (5 for such 10% ISO holders). Vesting as the Award Agreement
provides. **§422(d) $100,000** first-exercisable-per-year ISO limit (excess = NQSO). Exercise methods, post-
termination windows (e.g., 3 months; 12 months death/Disability; immediate for Cause), and §83(b) for early
exercise per the Award Agreement.

**6. RESTRICTED STOCK AWARDS.** Sale/grant of Shares subject to vesting and a repurchase/forfeiture restriction;
purchase price (if any) and §83(b) considerations per the Award Agreement.

**7. STOCK BONUS AWARDS.** Shares granted as a bonus for services, subject to any vesting/restrictions.

**8. STOCK APPRECIATION RIGHTS (SARs).** Right to the appreciation in FMV over the base price (≥ FMV at grant),
settled in Shares/cash; term ≤ 10 years.

**9. RESTRICTED STOCK UNITS (RSUs).** Unfunded promise to deliver Shares (or cash) on vesting/settlement;
settlement timing structured to be exempt from or compliant with **§409A**; no stockholder rights until settlement.

**10. PERFORMANCE AWARDS.** Awards conditioned on performance goals over a performance period set by the
Administrator; certification of achievement before payout.

**11. PAYMENT FOR SHARE PURCHASES.** Payment by cash/check, cancellation of indebtedness, surrender of Shares,
broker-assisted cashless exercise, net exercise, or other legal consideration the Administrator approves.

**12. GRANTS TO NON-EMPLOYEE DIRECTORS.** Permitted, subject to any annual director compensation limit **[$ / # cap]**.

**13. WITHHOLDING TAXES.** Issuance/settlement conditioned on satisfaction of all Tax-Related Items; the Company may
withhold Shares/cash up to the maximum statutory rate.

**14. TRANSFERABILITY.** Awards nontransferable except by will/descent (or as the Administrator permits / under a
DRO); exercisable during the holder's life only by the holder.

**15. PRIVILEGES OF STOCK OWNERSHIP.** No stockholder rights (dividends/voting) until Shares are issued; dividend
equivalents only if expressly granted.

**16. CERTIFICATES.** Shares may be certificated or book-entry; all bear required legends.

**17. ESCROW.** The Company may hold unvested/restricted Shares in escrow until vesting/release.

**18. SECURITIES LAW AND OTHER REGULATORY COMPLIANCE.** No Shares issued unless exempt/registered (Rule 701 /
§4(a)(2) / Form S-8 as applicable) and in compliance with the Securities Act, Exchange Act, and exchange rules;
issuance may be deferred to achieve compliance.

**19. NO OBLIGATION TO EMPLOY.** Nothing confers a right to continued service or limits the Company's right to
terminate.

**20. CORPORATE TRANSACTIONS.** On a Corporate Transaction/Change in Control, outstanding Awards may be assumed,
substituted, accelerated, cashed out, or terminated (if not exercised) per the Administrator and this Section.

**21. ADOPTION AND STOCKHOLDER APPROVAL.** Effective on board adoption; **stockholder approval within 12 months**
is required for ISO treatment and exchange rules; Awards granted before approval are contingent on it.

**22. TERM OF PLAN / GOVERNING LAW.** No Award granted after **10 years** from adoption/approval; the Plan, all
Awards, and all matters of corporate authority to authorize and issue the Shares are governed by the laws of the
**State of Texas** (including the Texas Business Organizations Code, Chapter 21), without regard to conflict-of-laws
principles, except that the federal Code, the Securities Act, and the Exchange Act govern the matters they address.

**23. AMENDMENT OR TERMINATION OF PLAN.** The board may amend/terminate the Plan; stockholder approval for material
amendments (and as exchange/§422 rules require); no amendment may materially impair an outstanding Award without
consent.

**24. NONEXCLUSIVITY OF THE PLAN.** The Plan does not limit the board's authority to adopt other compensation
arrangements.

**25. INSIDER TRADING POLICY.** Awards and transactions are subject to the Company's insider-trading policy and any
clawback/recoupment policy and applicable law.

**26. CANCELLATION OR RE-PRICING / EXCHANGE.** Re-pricing, cancellation-and-regrant, or exchange of underwater
Options/SARs requires **stockholder approval** except for adjustments under Section 20.

**27. SECTION 409A.** Awards are intended to be exempt from or comply with §409A and are construed/administered
accordingly; the Company may amend to maintain exemption/compliance and is not liable for any §409A tax.

**28. MISCELLANEOUS.** Severability; headings; electronic delivery/signature; data-privacy consent (incl. cross-
border transfer for award administration); unfunded/general-unsecured-creditor status; successors; entire plan;
no fractional Shares; foreign-jurisdiction sub-plans/appendices permitted.
