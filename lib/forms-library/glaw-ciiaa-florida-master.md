As a condition of, and in consideration of, my [employment / consulting / service] with **[COMPANY], INC.** (with
its parents/subsidiaries/affiliates, the "Company Group"), I agree:

**1. RELATIONSHIP / AT-WILL.** This Agreement applies to my entire period of service and survives its end. In
Florida, employment is **at-will** unless a separate written agreement says otherwise; nothing here alters that.

**2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION.** (a) I will hold the Company Group's Confidential Information in strict confidence,
use it only for the Company's benefit, and not disclose it. (b) "Confidential Information" = non-public technical,
business, financial, customer, and product information, and includes "trade secrets" as defined in the Florida
Uniform Trade Secrets Act, **Fla. Stat. §688.002**. (c) **Third-party / former-employer information:** I will not
use or disclose any third party's confidential information or trade secrets. (d) **Defend Trade Secrets Act notice
(18 U.S.C. §1833(b)):** immunity for disclosures to government/in a sealed court filing for reporting a suspected
legal violation. Nothing in this Agreement prohibits protected whistleblower disclosures or limits any right under
the Florida Whistleblower's Act, **Fla. Stat. §§448.101–448.105**.

**3. OWNERSHIP OF INVENTIONS.** (a) **Prior Inventions:** listed on **Exhibit A** are excluded; if none, I represent
there are none. (b) **Assignment of Inventions:** I assign to the Company all Inventions conceived/developed during
my service that relate to the Company's business or result from Company work/resources. (c) **Work Made for Hire:**
copyrightable works are "works made for hire"; to the extent not, I assign them. (d) **Moral Rights:** I waive any
moral rights to the fullest extent permitted. (e) **State-law carve-out:** Florida has **no** employee-invention
assignment-limiting statute (unlike Cal. Lab. Code §2870); the assignment in (b) is therefore enforced as written,
limited only by its express scope (relation to Company business / use of Company time or resources). If this person
also performs work in a state with such a statute (e.g., CA, IL, WA, NC, MN, DE, NJ, NV, UT), counsel must attach
the corresponding Exhibit B notice. (f) **Further assurances + Power of Attorney:** I will assist in
obtaining/enforcing IP and appoint the Company my attorney-in-fact to execute related documents.

**4. RETURN OF COMPANY PROPERTY.** On termination (or on request) I will return all Company property, documents, and
devices, and delete Company data from personal systems.

**5. NOTIFICATION TO NEW EMPLOYER.** The Company may notify a future employer of my obligations under this Agreement.

**6. RESTRICTIVE COVENANTS (Fla. Stat. §542.335).** I acknowledge that the Company Group has one or more
**legitimate business interests** justifying the covenants below, which may include: trade secrets; valuable
confidential business or professional information not rising to the level of a trade secret; substantial
relationships with specific prospective or existing customers, patients, or clients; customer/patient/client
goodwill associated with the Company's trade name, geographic location, or marketing area; and extraordinary or
specialized training. Each covenant is **reasonably necessary** to protect those interests and is reasonable as to
time, area, and line of business.

&nbsp;&nbsp;(a) **Non-solicitation of customers.** During service and for **[__] months** after, I will not, within
**[GEOGRAPHIC AREA / the area in which the Company does business]**, directly or indirectly solicit or service the
Company Group's existing or prospective customers, patients, or clients with whom I had material contact or about
whom I obtained Confidential Information.

&nbsp;&nbsp;(b) **Non-solicitation of personnel.** During service and for **[__] months** after, I will not solicit,
recruit, or hire away any employee, contractor, or consultant of the Company Group.

&nbsp;&nbsp;(c) **Non-competition.** During service and for **[__] months** after, within **[GEOGRAPHIC AREA]**, I
will not engage in or provide services to a business that competes with the Company Group in **[LINE OF BUSINESS]**.

&nbsp;&nbsp;(d) **Non-disparagement.** I will not make false or disparaging statements about the Company Group, its
products, officers, or directors; this does not restrict truthful testimony, protected disclosures, or statements to
a government agency.

&nbsp;&nbsp;(e) **Statutory presumptions (Fla. Stat. §542.335(1)(d)1).** For a former employee, agent, or
independent contractor, a post-term restraint of **6 months or less is presumed reasonable** and a restraint of
**more than 2 years is presumed unreasonable**; the duration in each covenant above is set within these bounds. A
court **shall construe** a restrictive covenant in favor of providing reasonable protection to the legitimate
business interest, **shall not construe** the covenant narrowly against the Company, and shall **modify
("blue-pencil")** any overbroad covenant and enforce it as modified, granting only the relief reasonably necessary
to protect the interest. A violation of an enforceable covenant creates a presumption of **irreparable injury**.

**7. REMEDIES.** Breach causes irreparable harm; the Company is entitled to **injunctive/equitable relief** under
**Fla. Stat. §542.335(1)(j)** plus other remedies, without posting bond where permitted, and to recover attorneys'
fees and costs to the extent provided by §542.335(1)(k).
