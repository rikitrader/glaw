This **SELECTED DEALER AGREEMENT** is between **[DEALER MANAGER], L.P.** (the "Dealer Manager") and the undersigned
broker-dealer (the "Selected Dealer"), relating to interests in **[FUND/ISSUER]**.

**1. APPOINTMENT.** The Dealer Manager appoints the Selected Dealer (a FINRA member in good standing) to solicit
purchases on a **best-efforts** basis, subject to the PPM and applicable law.
**2. OFFERING TERMS.** Offers only by the PPM; only to suitable accredited/qualified investors; no general
solicitation (or 506(c) verification); the Dealer follows the subscription procedures.
**3. SELLING COMMISSIONS / COMPENSATION.** Selling commission [__]%, dealer-manager fee [__]%, [trail/servicing fee];
payment timing; reallowance; bona-fide-services / FINRA Rule 2310 / 5110 compensation limits.
**4. REPRESENTATIONS — SELECTED DEALER.** FINRA membership; licensed reps; suitability + KYC/AML compliance; Reg
BI/fiduciary; delivery of the PPM; no unauthorized sales literature. **State broker-dealer registration:** the
Selected Dealer is registered as a **dealer**, and each of its associated persons who offers or sells the interests
is registered as an **agent**, under the **Texas Securities Act, Tex. Gov't Code, Title 12, Subtitle B, Chapter 4001**
(dealer/agent registration — see **§4001.101** et seq.), with the **Texas State Securities Board (administered by the
Securities Commissioner)**, or qualifies for an available exemption, and will offer and sell interests to Texas
residents only while so registered or exempt; the Selected Dealer will keep its Form BD and Form U4 filings current,
will comply with the Board's renewal and reporting requirements, and will promptly notify the Dealer Manager of any
lapse, suspension, or revocation of its Texas dealer or agent registration. The Dealer acknowledges that the
interests are sold in reliance on an available exemption under the Texas Securities Act (e.g., the private/limited-
offering exemption, **Tex. Gov't Code Ch. 4005**) or, for a federal-covered security, pursuant to the applicable
notice filing with the Texas State Securities Board, as the offering documents specify.
**5. REPRESENTATIONS — DEALER MANAGER.** Authorization; the PPM does not omit material facts.
**6. SUITABILITY / AML / OFAC.** The Selected Dealer makes the suitability determination, performs CIP/AML/OFAC, and
maintains records.
**7. INDEMNIFICATION & CONTRIBUTION.** Mutual indemnity for misstatements/violations; contribution fallback.
**8. CONDITIONS / TERMINATION.** Either party may terminate on [__] days' notice; survival of compensation earned +
indemnity.
**9. MISCELLANEOUS.** No partnership/authority beyond the agency; notices; **governing law — State of Texas, with
venue in [COUNTY] County, Texas, without regard to conflict-of-laws principles**, except that the Securities Act,
the Exchange Act, and FINRA rules govern the matters they address; counterparts / e-sign; entire agreement;
FINRA-required provisions.
