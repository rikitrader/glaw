# RIGHT OF FIRST REFUSAL AND CO-SALE AGREEMENT OF [COMPANY], INC. (A FLORIDA CORPORATION)

## 2. AGREEMENT AMONG THE COMPANY, THE INVESTORS, AND THE KEY HOLDERS
**2.1 Right of First Refusal.** Before any Proposed Transfer of Key Holder Stock, the transferring Key Holder shall deliver a Proposed Transfer Notice to the Company and the Investors. The **Company** shall have the first right to purchase all or any portion of the offered shares on the same terms set forth in the notice; to the extent the Company does not exercise its right in full, the **Investors** shall have a Secondary Refusal Right to purchase the remaining offered shares on a pro-rata basis, with an undersubscription (over-allotment) right, each within the applicable notice period. **2.2 Right of Co-Sale.** To the extent the offered shares are not purchased under § 2.1, each Investor may elect to participate ("tag along") in the Key Holder's Proposed Transfer on a pro-rata basis and on the same terms and conditions. **2.3 Effect of Failure to Comply.** Any Proposed Transfer made in violation of this Agreement shall be void; the Company shall not record any such transfer on its books; and the transferring Key Holder shall, at the election of the Investors, be subject to put and other remedy mechanics and shall indemnify the participating Investors for any related losses.

## 3. EXEMPT TRANSFERS
**3.1 Exempted Transfers.** The Right of First Refusal and the Right of Co-Sale shall not apply to a transfer to a permitted transferee (including for estate-planning purposes, to a trust for the benefit of the Key Holder or immediate family, or to an Affiliate), provided the transferee takes the shares subject to this Agreement and executes the joinder required by § 3.3. **3.2 Exempted Offerings** (the rights do not apply to shares sold in a registered public offering or repurchased by the Company). **3.3 Transferees.** Any permitted transferee shall, as a condition to the transfer, execute a joinder agreement and thereby become bound by this Agreement.

## 4. LEGEND
Each certificate, instrument, or book-entry position representing shares subject to this Agreement shall bear a legend referencing the transfer restrictions set forth herein, consistent with § 607.0627, Fla. Stat.

## 5. LOCK-UP
**5.1 Agreement to Lock-Up.** Each holder agrees that, for a period of up to **180 days** following the effective date of the Company's initial public offering (the market stand-off period), it will not, without the consent of the managing underwriters, sell or otherwise transfer any Capital Stock, and it will execute the underwriters' customary lock-up agreement. **5.2 Stop-Transfer Instructions.** The Company may impose stop-transfer instructions, and may legend the affected shares, to enforce the lock-up.

## 6. RESTRICTIONS ON TRANSFER
**6.1 Restrictions on Transfer.** No holder shall transfer any Capital Stock except in compliance with the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (whether pursuant to an effective registration statement or an available exemption), applicable state securities laws, and this Agreement. **6.2 Transfer Requirements.** As a condition to any transfer, the Company may reasonably require an opinion of counsel, evidence of Rule 144 compliance, and customary representations from the transferee.
