## 2. AGREEMENT AMONG THE COMPANY, THE INVESTORS AND THE KEY HOLDERS
**2.1 Right of First Refusal.** Before any Proposed Transfer of Key Holder Stock, the Key Holder delivers a Proposed
Transfer Notice; the **Company** has the first right to purchase the offered shares on the same terms, then the
**Investors** have a Secondary Refusal Right (pro-rata, with an undersubscription/over-allotment), within the notice
period. **2.2 Right of Co-Sale.** To the extent the offered shares are not purchased under 2.1, each Investor may
participate ("tag along") in the Key Holder's sale pro-rata on the same terms. **2.3 Effect of Failure to Comply.**
A transfer in violation is void; the Company will not record it; put/remedy mechanics; the Key Holder indemnifies.

## 3. EXEMPT TRANSFERS
**3.1 Exempted Transfers.** ROFR/Co-Sale do not apply to transfers to permitted transferees (estate planning,
trusts, affiliates) who take subject to this Agreement. **3.2 Exempted Offerings** (registered offerings / Company
repurchases). **3.3 Transferees.** Any permitted transferee executes a joinder and is bound.

## 4. LEGEND
Certificates/book-entries bear the legend referencing this Agreement's transfer restrictions.

## 5. LOCK-UP
**5.1 Agreement to Lock-Up.** Holders agree not to sell for up to **180 days** following the Company's IPO (market
stand-off), and to sign the underwriters' lock-up. **5.2 Stop-Transfer Instructions.** The Company may impose
stop-transfer instructions to enforce the lock-up.

## 6. RESTRICTIONS ON TRANSFER
**6.1 Restrictions on Transfer.** No transfer except in compliance with the Securities Act (registration or
exemption) and this Agreement. **6.2 Transfer Requirements.** Opinion of counsel / Rule 144 compliance / transferee
representations as the Company reasonably requires.
