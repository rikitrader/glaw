As a condition of, and in consideration of, my [employment / consulting / service] with **[COMPANY], INC.** (with
its parents/subsidiaries/affiliates, the "Company Group"), I agree:

**1. RELATIONSHIP / AT-WILL.** This Agreement applies to my entire period of service and survives its end. [In the
US, employment is **at-will** unless a separate written agreement says otherwise; nothing here alters that.]

**2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION.** (a) I will hold the Company Group's Confidential Information in strict confidence,
use it only for the Company's benefit, and not disclose it. (b) "Confidential Information" = non-public technical,
business, financial, customer, and product information. (c) **Third-party / former-employer information:** I will not
use or disclose any third party's confidential information or trade secrets. (d) **Defend Trade Secrets Act notice
(18 U.S.C. §1833(b)):** immunity for disclosures to government/in a sealed court filing for reporting a suspected
legal violation.

**3. OWNERSHIP OF INVENTIONS.** (a) **Prior Inventions:** listed on **Exhibit A** are excluded; if none, I represent
there are none. (b) **Assignment of Inventions:** I assign to the Company all Inventions conceived/developed during
my service that relate to the Company's business or result from Company work/resources. (c) **Work Made for Hire:**
copyrightable works are "works made for hire"; to the extent not, I assign them. (d) **Moral Rights:** I waive any
moral rights to the fullest extent permitted. (e) **State-law carve-out:** assignment excludes inventions that
qualify under the applicable state statute (e.g., Cal. Lab. Code §2870) developed entirely on my own time without
Company resources and unrelated to its business — Exhibit B notice. (f) **Further assurances + Power of Attorney:**
I will assist in obtaining/enforcing IP and appoint the Company my attorney-in-fact to execute related documents.

**4. RETURN OF COMPANY PROPERTY.** On termination (or on request) I will return all Company property, documents, and
devices, and delete Company data from personal systems.

**5. NOTIFICATION TO NEW EMPLOYER.** The Company may notify a future employer of my obligations under this Agreement.

**6. RESTRICTIVE COVENANTS.** During service and for [__] months after, I will not (to the extent enforceable in the
governing state): (a) **solicit** Company employees/contractors or customers; (b) [**non-compete** — INCLUDE ONLY
where enforceable; void/limited in CA, MN, ND, OK and restricted elsewhere — counsel to confirm]. Covenants are
reasonable and severable/blue-penciled to the maximum enforceable scope.

**7. REMEDIES.** Breach causes irreparable harm; the Company is entitled to **injunctive/equitable relief** plus
other remedies, without posting bond where permitted.

**8. GENERAL.** Entire agreement (with any offer letter/RSPA/plan); no oral modification; assignable by the Company;
binds successors/heirs; severability/blue-pencil; **governing law [state]**; counterparts / electronic signature;
**spousal/community-property** acknowledgment where applicable.
