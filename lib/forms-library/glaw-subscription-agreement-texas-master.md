# SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

> **Texas variant.** Governing law and forum are Texas. The offering is conducted under the **Texas Securities Act, Tex. Gov't Code Ch. 4001** (administered by the Texas State Securities Board); the Texas blue-sky representation and legend (§ 4A) apply. Texas is a **community-property state**, so an individual Subscriber's spouse must execute the community-property acknowledgment (§ 4B). Any provision turning on a state's entity statute must be reviewed by counsel against the Texas Business Organizations Code (TBOC).
