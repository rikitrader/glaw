# SHARED CAPTION
```
IN THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR THE [DISTRICT] DISTRICT OF [STATE]
[PLAINTIFF], Plaintiff, v. [DEFENDANT], Defendant.        Case No.: ____
```

## FORM 1 — SUBPOENA TO TESTIFY AT A TRIAL/HEARING (Rule 45 — AO 88)
TO: [WITNESS, ADDRESS]. YOU ARE COMMANDED to appear in the United States District Court at [COURTHOUSE]
on [DATE] at [TIME] to testify at [the trial / hearing] in this case. **Place limits (Rule 45(c)(1)):**
within 100 miles of where the person resides, is employed, or regularly transacts business — or anywhere
in the state if a party/officer or the travel is not a substantial burden. Witness fee + mileage must be
tendered (Rule 45(b)(1); 28 U.S.C. § 1821).

## FORM 2 — SUBPOENA TO TESTIFY AT A DEPOSITION (Rule 45; with a Rule 30 notice)
TO: [DEPONENT, ADDRESS]. YOU ARE COMMANDED to appear at [LOCATION / remote platform] on [DATE] at
[TIME] to testify at a deposition. **Deposition place must comply with the 100-mile rule (Rule
45(c)(1)).** [Organization: serve a Rule 30(b)(6) notice listing the matters; the entity designates a
witness.] [Add "and bring the documents in Attachment A" for a deposition duces tecum.]

## FORM 3 — SUBPOENA TO PRODUCE DOCUMENTS / ESI (duces tecum to a NON-PARTY, Rule 45)
**PREREQUISITE (Rule 45(a)(4)):** before serving, serve a **notice + a copy** of the subpoena on every
other party.
TO: [NON-PARTY custodian — e.g., [BANK] Records Custodian, ADDRESS]. YOU ARE COMMANDED to **produce**,
on or before [DATE], at [ATTORNEY ADDRESS] (or by sending legible/native copies), the documents and ESI
in **Attachment A** [Production may be at a place within 100 miles of the non-party — Rule 45(c)(2)].
**Attachment A — documents requested:** 1. [All records concerning account/matter [____], [date] to
[date]]. 2. [Account-opening/signature records]. 3. [Wire/ACH/transaction records]. 4. [Contracts/
communications with [party]]. A **business-records certification** (FRE 902(11)) is requested in lieu of
appearance. The recipient may serve **objections within 14 days** (Rule 45(d)(2)(B)); the issuing party
must take reasonable steps to avoid imposing **undue burden** (Rule 45(d)(1)) and protect privileged/
trial-prep material.

**RULE 45(d)(3) — the court MUST quash/modify** a subpoena that fails to allow reasonable time, requires
travel beyond the 100-mile limit, requires disclosure of privileged matter, or subjects a person to undue
burden.
