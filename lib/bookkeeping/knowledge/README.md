# GLAW Accounting Knowledge Base

An **original**, plain-English reference for the bookkeeping, accounting, and CPA
fundamentals the GLAW finance agents (`/glaw-ledger`, `/glaw-controller`, `/glaw-cfo`,
`/glaw-audit`, `/glaw-bookkeeping`) are grounded in. Written from general professional
knowledge — **not copied from any copyrighted dictionary or course**. Definitions are
tied to how GLAW actually posts and proves the numbers, so they are practical, not abstract.

## Contents
1. [Foundations](01-foundations.md) — debits/credits, the accounting equation, double-entry, accounts, journals, ledgers.
2. [The accounting cycle & close](02-cycle-and-close.md) — transaction → JE → post → trial balance → adjust → close → lock.
3. [Financial statements & accounts](03-statements-and-accounts.md) — the four statements, account types, the chart of accounts, subledgers.
4. [Accruals, depreciation & tax](04-accruals-depreciation-tax.md) — cash vs accrual, deferrals, depreciation methods, payroll, sales tax.
5. [Controls, reconciliation & ratios](05-controls-ratios-audit.md) — internal controls, bank rec, audit trail, the key ratios.

## How the agents use it
- `/glaw-ledger` posts every term here that is an **entry** (debit/credit, JE, adjusting entry).
- `/glaw-controller` runs the **cycle** (file 2) and ties the **subledgers** (file 3).
- `/glaw-cfo` reads the **statements + ratios** (files 3, 5) and runs the review loop.
- `/glaw-audit` proves the **controls + reconciliation + trail** (file 5).

> Not legal, tax, or accounting advice. A grounding reference for attorney/CPA-reviewed work-product.
