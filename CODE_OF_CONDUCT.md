# Code of Conduct

GLAW follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone. Be
respectful, assume good faith, critique ideas not people, and keep discussion
focused on building a better firm. Unacceptable behavior — harassment, personal
attacks, sharing others' private information — may result in removal from the
project.

Report concerns by opening a private issue or contacting a maintainer. Maintainers
will review and respond in good faith.
