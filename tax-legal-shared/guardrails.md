# Shared Tax/Legal Guardrails

These rules apply to every GLAW tax, accounting, corporate, IRS, SEC, and
financial-reporting workflow.

1. Source first: every tax/legal number must cite `current-figures.md` or be held
   for review.
2. Human seal: filing, signature, service, payment, charge, or live transmission
   requires the human-authority gate.
3. No legal advice claim: deliverables are attorney/CPA work-product for licensed
   review unless an authorized professional adopts them.
4. Red flags survive until sourced: unsupported amounts, broken tie-outs, missing
   forms, and unverified deadlines remain open red flags.
5. Bank-to-tax tie-out: tax outputs using books data must tie to source bank
   statements, ledger workpapers, reconciliation reports, and tax tie-out
   artifacts.
