# Sources & Authority Index — SEC Financial Reporting Seat

This KB rests on **primary authority**: the **Securities Act of 1933**, the **Securities Exchange
Act of 1934**, **Regulation S-X**, **Regulation S-K**, the **SEC forms and their instructions**, the
governing **SEC rules**, and the SEC Division of Corporation Finance **Financial Reporting Manual**
(a public SEC work). Practice guides inform literacy but are never the operative rule. **Every
filer-status threshold, deadline, and fee defers to `tax-legal-shared/current-figures.md` or the
current SEC rule** — they change.

## Verification posture

Statutes verified against law.cornell.edu / govinfo.gov; regulations against the eCFR (Parts 210
and 229); forms, deadlines, filer-status thresholds, 13D/13G timing, and Inline-XBRL scope against
**sec.gov** — these are the volatile inputs and the SEC amends them. When web tools are available,
verify the specific rule, item number, and deadline in play. Where a section/item number is
uncertain, name the requirement and the regulation rather than guess a number.
