# Geographic Targeting Order (GTO) Tracker

## What a GTO is
A **Geographic Targeting Order** (31 U.S.C. **5326**; 31 CFR 1010.370) is a time-limited
FinCEN order imposing **additional reporting/recordkeeping** on specified businesses in a
**specified geographic area**, typically by **lowering a reporting threshold** to surface
illicit cash flows. GTOs are temporary, renewable, and frequently **modified** — so the
operative terms must always be read from the **current** order, not a prior one.

> **Volatility warning.** ZIP codes, dollar thresholds, covered-business definitions, and
> expiration dates change with each modification. **Confirm the live terms on FinCEN.gov
> before advising any covered business.** The entries below are a chronology, not the
> operative thresholds.

## 1. Southwest Border GTO (money services businesses)
Targets **bulk-cash / cartel** activity by lowering the CTR threshold for **MSBs** in
specified Southwest-border ZIP codes (well below the $10,000 standard).

| Date | Event | Type |
|---|---|---|
| 2025-03-11 | Original Southwest Border GTO issued (corpus) | GTO |
| 2025-03-24 / 04-09 / 04-16 | FAQs + FinCEN Exchange sessions (corpus) | FAQ |
| **2025-09-08** | **Modified** Southwest Border GTO | GTO |
| **2026-03-10** | **Expanded** Southwest Border GTO | GTO |
| **2026-03-19** | **FAQs** on the Southwest Border GTO | FAQ |

- **Authority:** 31 U.S.C. 5326; 31 CFR 1010.370.
- **Covered:** money services businesses in the listed ZIP codes.
- **Current terms (confirmed for the expanded order):** CTR threshold lowered to **$1,000**
  (original March 2025 order: **$200**), covering MSBs in specified counties/ZIP codes in
  **Arizona, California, New Mexico, and Texas** near the Southwest border, effective
  **March 7 – September 2, 2026**; CTR filing window extended to 30 days. New-coverage MSBs
  had a 30-day compliance grace (filing began April 6, 2026).
- **Compliance impact:** file CTRs at the **GTO's lower threshold** for the order's duration;
  keep the required records. Confirm covered ZIPs + exact threshold from the current order.
- **Related:** 2025-12-22 data-driven border ML operation; 2025-06/08 fentanyl-counter orders
  (extended Aug 2025) — separate authorities but same border-enforcement theme.

## 2. Minnesota (Hennepin / Ramsey) Fraud GTO (money transmitters & banks)
Responds to large-scale **public-program fraud** in the Twin Cities metro.

| Date | Event | Type |
|---|---|---|
| 2026-01-09 | Sec. Bessent announces Minnesota anti-fraud initiatives | Notice |
| 2026-01-13 | FAQs on the Minnesota Fraud GTO | FAQ |
| **2026-02-27** | **Limited Exemptive Relief Order** for affected FIs | Exceptive Relief |
| **2026-04-09** | **Updated FAQs** on the Hennepin/Ramsey GTO | FAQ |

- **Authority:** 31 U.S.C. 5326; 31 CFR 1010.370; exemptive relief under 5318(a).
- **Covered:** money transmitters and banks in **Hennepin and Ramsey Counties, MN**.
- **Compliance impact:** comply with the GTO's reporting/recordkeeping terms; **read the
  2026-02-27 exemptive-relief order** to determine whether your institution qualifies for the
  limited carve-out. Confirm operative terms on FinCEN.gov.
