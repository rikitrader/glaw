# AML / Sanctions Whistleblower Program

## A. The program
- **Authority:** created by **AMLA-2020 § 6314** and strengthened by the **AML Whistleblower
  Improvement Act of 2022**; codified at **31 U.S.C. 5323**.
- **What it covers:** original-information tips about violations of the **BSA** and of
  **U.S. economic sanctions** programs (IEEPA / TWEA / OFAC-administered sanctions).
- **Awards:** an eligible whistleblower whose information leads to a successful enforcement
  action with monetary sanctions **over $1,000,000** may receive **10%–30%** of the collected
  amount. A dedicated whistleblower fund backs awards (no longer subject to the original
  unfunded/uncapped uncertainty after the 2022 amendments).
- **Anti-retaliation:** the statute prohibits employer retaliation against whistleblowers and
  provides remedies.

## B. 2026-02-13 — FinCEN Launches Whistleblower Tips Webpage `Notice`
- **Substance:** FinCEN stood up a dedicated **webpage for whistleblower tips** on **fraud,
  money laundering, and sanctions violations** — the intake channel for the program.
- **Compliance impact (two-sided):**
  - **For institutions:** insiders now have a **funded, anti-retaliation channel** to report
    AML/sanctions failures directly to the government. This raises the stakes for internal AML
    controls, the seriousness of unaddressed escalations, and culpable-conduct exposure.
    Strengthen internal reporting so issues are caught and fixed **before** they leave the
    building. Treat retaliation as its own liability.
  - **For potential whistleblowers:** a real award + anti-retaliation framework exists — but
    eligibility, the "original information" requirement, and process are technical. Route any
    actual whistleblower decision to **licensed counsel** (this is not a filing this seat makes;
    cf. `/glaw-irs-whistleblower` and `/glaw-sec-whistleblower` for the parallel programs).

## C. How GLAW uses this
- This is a **doctrine/posture** file, not a filing engine. GLAW does not file a whistleblower
  tip; it explains exposure and the program, and routes the decision to counsel.
- Relevant to enforcement-risk assessments: where the AML investigation (`/glaw-fincen-aml`) or
  SAR analysis (`/glaw-fincen-sar`) surfaces institutional failures, note the heightened
  whistleblower exposure in the report.
