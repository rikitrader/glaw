# Sources & Authority Index — Sales & Use Tax / VAT Seat

This KB rests on **primary authority**: each state's **sales-and-use-tax statute and
regulations**, the controlling **U.S. Supreme Court** Commerce-Clause cases, the **Streamlined
Sales and Use Tax Agreement (SSUTA)**, and **Multistate Tax Commission (MTC)** materials. Practice
guides inform literacy but are never the operative rule. **Every dollar / transaction threshold,
rate, fee, and discount defers to `tax-legal-shared/current-figures.md` and to the state's current
rate table** — there is no national figure.

## State law (the operative rule — pattern, not a single citation)

Sales/use tax is imposed and defined **by each state**, so the operative authority is always the
**specific state's** statute, regulation, and DOR guidance. The recurring structure to locate in
each state:

- **Imposition statute** — the tax on retail sales of tangible personal property (and enumerated
  services); the **use-tax** complement.
- **Nexus statute** — the post-*Wayfair* **economic-nexus** provision (dollar / transaction
  threshold, measurement base, lookback) and any **marketplace-facilitator**, **click-through**,
  and **affiliate** provisions.
- **Taxability** — the enumerated-services list, **digital-goods / SaaS** treatment, and
  **bundling** rules.
- **Exemptions & certificates** — resale, manufacturing, agricultural, nonprofit/governmental
  exemptions and the **certificate** requirements.
- **Sourcing** — destination vs. origin for intrastate and interstate sales; the combined
  state + local rate table.
- **Administration** — registration/permit, filing frequency, vendor collection allowance,
  successor/bulk-sale clearance, responsible-person liability, audit/sampling, VDA, and amnesty.

**Do not assert a state's rule from memory.** Name the controlling statute/regulation/ruling and
verify the current text on the state DOR's site.

## Streamlined Sales Tax (SSUTA)

- **Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement** — uniform definitions, uniform exemption
  certificate, simplified rate/sourcing, single registration (SSTRS), and **Certified Service
  Providers (CSPs)** (state-funded for volunteer sellers). Administered by the **Streamlined Sales
  Tax Governing Board** (streamlinedsalestax.org). Member-state list and CSP program change —
  confirm before relying. | registration-collection-remittance.md

## Multistate Tax Commission (MTC)

- **Uniform Sales & Use Tax Exemption / Resale Certificate** — a multistate certificate accepted
  by many (not all) states; conditions vary. | taxability-and-exemptions.md
- **MTC statement on P.L. 86-272 and internet activities** — the position that many online
  activities exceed mere solicitation (relevant to the income-tax seat, not a sales-tax shield).
  | nexus-and-economic-nexus.md

## Verification posture

Supreme Court holdings verified against the U.S. Reports / supremecourt.gov; the SSUTA against
streamlinedsalestax.org; the MTC certificate against mtc.gov. **State statutes, rates,
thresholds, and filing frequencies must be re-confirmed against the specific state DOR before
relying** — they are the most volatile inputs in this practice. When web tools are available,
verify the specific state rule and the current threshold in play.
